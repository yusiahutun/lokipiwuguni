Where to Watch New Years Eve Live Nashvilles Big Bash For Free 2023: How to Stream

If you’re spending the New Year cozy at home, you might want to know where to watch New Year’s Eve Live Nashville’s Big Bash for free to liven up the party.

The annual show takes place in Nashville Downtown’s Bicentennial Capitol Mall State Park. The Big Bash will kick off more than 50 back-to-back performances, live from the home of country music. It’ll also feature once-in-a-lifetime collaborations with artists inviting special guests and artist friends to perform together throughout the night.

The New Year’s Eve Live Nashville’s Big Bash special first aired in 2021 after CBS wanted to produce a country-oriented show. Nashville will count down with the East Coast at midnight, and will continue the festivities through midnight Central Time with Nashville’s Music Note Drop.

When does New Year’s Eve Live Nashville’s Big Bash air?

New Year’s Eve Live Nashville’s Big Bash airs on December 31, 2023, at 7:30 p.m. on CBS. There will be a break for local news at 9 p.m. and will restart at 10 p.m.
