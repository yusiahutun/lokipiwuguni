Kate Bosworth, Zoe Kravitz and Kendall Jenner: The best Coachella outfits of all time

The Coachella Valley Music And Arts Festival is a close contender to Glastonbury when it comes to global reputation and a star-studded guest list. 

The festival is held at the Empire Polo Club in Indio, California. The location is only a two-hour drive from Los Angeles and about 30 minutes from the glitzy Palm Springs. As one of the world's most reknown festivals, it has become a fixture on the calendar of some of our favourite fashion icons and influencers. As a result, it has played host to some incredible outfits.

When was the first ever Coachella?

The inaugural Coachella Valley Music and Arts Festival was held on October 9–10, 1999. Headlining the event were Beck, Tool, and Rage Against the Machine.

With Coachella 2023 kicking off on Friday April 14 (and headliners including Bad Bunny, Blackpink and Frank Ocean) we take a look back at the most memorable and amazing Coachella fashion over the years...

The 20 best Coachella outfits of all time:

Kate Bosworth:

Kate Bosworth was pictured with then-husband Michael Polish at Coachella on April 11, 2015 in one of the most memorable festival looks of all time.

Alessandra Ambrosio:

Alessandra Ambrosio attended Coachella 2022 in a white lace jumpsuit. 

Beyonce:

Beyonce Knowles rocked a indie-sleaze aesthetic during Day 1 of Coachella back in 2010.

Rihanna:

Rihanna in the audience during Day 2 of Coachella 2011 wearing a camo jacket.

Camila Coelho:

Camila Coelho attended Coachella 2019 on April 13, wearing a navy and white patterned co-ord and rope belt. 

Chiara Ferragni:

Chiara Ferragni wore a sheer dress on day 3 of Coachella 2017.

Aimee Song:

 Aimee Song wore a white maxi skirt, sandals and floral blouse at Coachella 2019.

Sienna Miller and Poppy Delevingne:

Actress Sienna Miller and supermodel Poppy Delevingne attended the Superdry Coachella brunch hosted by the latter on April 12, 2014.

Chanel Iman:

Chanel wore a folk inspired dress, and rocked colourful braids at Coachella 2017.

Devon Windsor:

Devon Windsor photographed during day 3 of Coachella 2018 wearing an embroidered shirt and white shorts.

Zoë Kravitz:

Zoë Kravitz attended Coachella on April 11, 2015 wearing Marc by Marc Jacobs sunglasses and a flannel plaid dress.

Kendall Jenner:

Kendall Jenner was seen in a crochet dress and space buns on April 15, 2016 at Coachella.

Gigi Hadid:

Gigi Hadid was seen at Coachella on April 12, 2015 in a white dress paired with a brown leather belt and silk paisley bandana scarf.

Lais Ribeiro:

Model Lais Ribeiro wore a Victoria Secret bustier and fringed trousers during day 1 of Coachella 2018.

Jasmine Tookes:

Jasmine Tookes wore a Victoria Secret bra top and floral maxi skirt on April 13 at Coachella 2018.

Jourdan Dunn:

Jourdan Dunn was seen at Coachella on April 15, 2017 in white shorts and a printed tank top. 

Gizele Oliveira:

Model Gizele Oliveira wore a bucket hat, cropped top, sheer net pants at the Revolve Festival during Coachella on April 13, 2019.

Rachel Barnes:

Influencer Rachel Barnes attended the Cabana Club Pool Party  at Coachella on April 11, 2015 in a turquoise necklace and sheer dress.

Bella Hadid and Hailey Bieber:

Bella Hadid and Hailey Bieber attended the Levis Coachella brunch on 14 Apr 2018 wearing retro inspired outfits.

Brittany Xavier:

Fashion influencer Brittany Xavier schooled us on how to wear quirky hair accessories at a festival back in 2019 at Coachella.
