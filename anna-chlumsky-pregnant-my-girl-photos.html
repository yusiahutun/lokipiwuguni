&quot;My Girl&quot; Star Anna Chlumsky Pregnant -- See Her Baby Bump!

"My Girl" star Anna Chlumsky is about to have a girl (or boy!) of her own.

The former child star, now 32, just revealed she's pregnant with her first child. Does that make you feel old? Yeah, us too.

Chlumsky, who played the precocious Vada Sultenfuss in the 1991 classic opposite little Macaulay Culkin, put her growing baby bump on display Wednesday night on "Watch What Happens Live."

The child will be the first for Anna and husband of five years, Shaun So.

While on WWHL, the "Veep" star talked about her pregnancy style -- and how it'll differ from Kim Kardashian's.

"My plan is to just get as much kind of cheap, crazy looking things as possible," she told Andy Cohen.

"I went on an H&M rampage the other and bought crazy leggings with leopards on them," Anna added. "I'm taking advantage at this time to be insane."
