Photos Of The Charlies Angels Star Then &amp; Now Hollywood Life

Cheryl Ladd, 71, is a legendary actress in Hollywood. She is most known for playing the character Kris Munroe in the hit TV series, Charlie’s Angels, from 1976 to 1981. She has also starred in Romance on the Orient Express, Grace Kelly, Unforgettable, and many more. In April 2022, Cheryl also appeared in the Netflix film A Cowgirl’s Song.

Cheryl has been married to music producer Brian Russell since 1981, and the pair share two beautiful adult daughters together. Lindsay Russell is Brian’s daughter from a previous marriage, and Cheryl gave birth to her own daughter, Jordan Ladd, in 1975. The actress welcomed Jordan with her ex-husband, David Ladd.

In this photo, Cheryl Ladd is seen at the premiere of the film Unforgettable at the Chinese Theatre in Los Angeles on April 18, 2017. In the film, Cheryl plays the character Lovey, Helen alongside actress Katherine Heigl.
