Meaning of Kill em With Success by ScHoolboy Q

The ScHoolboy Q song titled 'Kill ’em With Success' is a powerful anthem that explores the concept of achieving success and using it as a weapon against those who doubt or underestimate you. The song features lyrics from Eearz, ScHoolboy Q, and 2 Chainz, each bringing their unique perspectives and styles to the track.One standout lyric from the song is "Ordinary, I am not that. They think I'm blasphemous, tattoos all on the neck." This lyric speaks to the notion of breaking away from societal expectations and embracing one's individuality. It suggests that success often comes to those who are unafraid to challenge conventions and stand out from the crowd. It further develops the theme of self-confidence and authenticity as tools for achieving success.Another noteworthy lyric is "Made the Forbes, needed more". This line delves into the idea that success is not necessarily a destination but rather a continuous journey. It suggests that even when one achieves a significant milestone, there is always a desire to strive for more. The lyric taps into the theme of ambition and the relentless pursuit of success, emphasizing the mindset that propels individuals to continue pushing boundaries and reaching new heights.The line "Nigga ball like the Mavs, finger-roll from the half" introduces a theme of resilience and adaptability. It likens the act of balling, or excelling, to the athleticism and agility displayed by players in a basketball game. The metaphorical finger-roll from the half captures the ability to navigate difficult situations with finesse and precision, even when faced with seemingly insurmountable odds. This lyric underscores the notion that success requires not only skill and talent but also the ability to think quickly, adapt to challenges, and make the best out of any situation.The lyric "I put the mind in determine, I put the mind on a journey" explores the power of determination and mindset in achieving success. It suggests that one's mental attitude and resilience play crucial roles in overcoming obstacles and achieving greatness. By highlighting the importance of mindset, this lyric adds another layer to the song's thematic exploration of success as a mindset and an attitude rather than just a tangible achievement.Overall, 'Kill ’em With Success' delves deep into the multifaceted nature of success and the various themes that surround it. It celebrates individuality, ambition, resilience, adaptability, and the power of mindset. The song reminds listeners of the potential within themselves to achieve greatness and challenges them to use their success as a weapon against those who doubt or underestimate them. It serves as a powerful anthem, encouraging individuals to embrace their unique qualities and push past boundaries in their pursuit of success.
