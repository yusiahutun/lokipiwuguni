Jason Kelce can't remember iconic 'Hamcuzzi' episode but has fond memories of a ham sandwich

Jason Kelce opened up over his ‘hamcuzzi’ throwback photo that has football fans in stitches. Who’d thought we be treated to a picture of the center in a jacuzzi? Well, we are in luck.

On the latest podcast episode of New Heights, which Jason Kelce hosts with his brother Travis, they talk about funny tweets and memes from fans – the 92%ers. Travis Kelce brought up one throwback photo someone had posted on Twitter, that sees the Philadelphia Eagles star in a jacuzzi but with a twist.

Jason Kelce’s ‘hamcuzzi’ photo is everything

On Wednesday, February 21, the celebrity brothers were talking during their Fan Mention from the week after their ‘New News’ segment. They discuss the latest happenings of the previous week, as well as memes, funny tweets and commentary from their fans.

This is where we got to see the Jason Kelce ‘hamcuzzi’ photo again, and it’s definitely a highlight of the past 7 days.

After Travis and the Kansas City Chiefs won the Super Bowl – and enjoyed the kiss with girlfriend Taylor Swift on the field – it may be easier to think nothing else will be worth watching until the season picks up again in the fall. But we were all wrong. Thankfully.

Travis says: “Our last Fan Mention from the Week comes from Chuck Knoll on Twitter, who posted an incredible photo of Jason [Kelce], let’s talk about the Hamcuzzi episode”.

The 36-year-old replies: “That Hamcuzzi episode, that was a long time ago, that is my rookie year I think? Jesus. Actually, that might be my second year for sure, that’s when I had the mustache.

“No, that’s all I got”.

Travis jokes his brother is ‘psychotic’

Travis then chimes in, upset he won’t talk about it anymore and asks him to at least explain the hilarious photo.

Jason Kelce adds: “It’s just eating a ham sandwich in a jacuzzi.”

“You get into the hot tub with a long sleeve shirt on,” Travis jokes, saying he’s “psychotic” for wearing a sweatshirt in the water.

Jason responds: “It’s a dry fit, there’s no world where you’ve seen a fat guy get in a poll with a shirt on?

“Well it does when somebody’s trying to take a picture of you.”

“Since when have you been ashamed of you r body?” The 34-year-old laughs.

“I don’t know,” Jason says. “I don’t know why I’m wearing that shirt, this happened over 10 years ago at this point

“I remember the ham sandwich was good and I remember I need to go in the jacuzzi with that thing. It looks toasted, it looks good.”

Jason then jokes people should get a ham sandwich and enjoy it in a hot tub “do yourself a favor”, before Travis interrupts with “or don’t”.

Here it is, to enjoy.
