The 12 Best Seamless Leggings for 2024

If you, like us, live in leggings, yet haven’t tried seamless pairs yet, we urge you to buy a pair right now. Because they provide a perfect fit with limited irritation or chafing, as you might get from more prominent seams, many people would rather grab a pair of seamless leggings over anything else. While seamless leggings aren’t actually entirely seamless, they have fewer seams and are made from one piece of fabric rather than several stitched together. That way, they can be more flexible and comfortable as you go about your daily activities.

Since seamless leggings offer so many advantages, we looked into which styles are the best of the best — giving you a smooth look and comfy support that’s just as suited to lounging as it is to sweat it out in the gym. We spoke to a range of professional fashion stylists with first-hand experience of what makes good leggings great, and we rounded up our favorites that you can shop right now.

Best Overall

Spanx EcoCare High-Waisted Seamless Leggings

Spanx

What We Love: They provide hold around the tummy and support for the legs, too.

What We Don’t Love: They run a little small.

Spanx has upgraded their much-loved Look at Me Now seamless leggings by keeping the winning design and making them in a more planet-friendly fabric, the recycled Sensil EcoCare nylon. UK-based stylist Emma Lightbown notes that these are “loved by loads of celebrities including Reese Witherspoon and Jennifer Garner, and offer compression without feeling restrictive.” Meanwhile, personal stylist and fashion blogger Tara West loves their ultra high-waisted cut for shaping your silhouette. They’re “designed to hit just below the bust for great coverage as the compression fabric smooths your waist and legs,” West says. “The seamless legging allows for an elegant look and unmatched comfort in any occasion.”

These leggings are perfection if you like a little more hold around your tummy, and they don’t roll down at all, which is a huge plus. The compression also allows your legs to feel supported, meaning they’ll feel less worn out after a long day out and about. Though some people do find these run small, the inclusive range of sizes should hopefully help many different people achieve their perfect fit. Last but not least, if you’re on the market for a more traditional waistline, stylist and creative consultant Lauren Glazer also loves the slightly lower-waisted version of these leggings.

Size: XS-3X | Length: Ankle | Colors: 1 | Material: EcoCare nylon

Best Budget

On that note, Boody is a certified B Corporation and aims to reduce carbon emissions and energy expenditure throughout their production process — which is extra cool if you consider it’s a more wallet-friendly brand as well.

What to Keep in Mind

Length

Leggings come in a few different lengths, so it’s best to have some idea of what length you like best. “Leggings are available in various lengths, such as full-length, capri, or ankle-length,” says Lightbown. “Choose a length that suits your comfort and the purpose for which you'll be wearing them.” But if you have no idea what length to opt for, West offers this insight. “I suggest an ankle-length legging, as they will make you look chic and confident with any style shoe,” she says.

Compression

When choosing seamless leggings, it’s important to decide how much compression you’re looking for, depending on what you’re planning to use yours for. “Some leggings offer a high level of compression, which can provide support and help with muscle recovery during workouts,” Lightbown says. “Others may have a lighter compression for everyday wear. Determine your desired level of compression based on your activities and personal preferences.”

Rosales also notes that you should pay attention to sizing when buying high-compression leggings. “If you go too small, the material will roll when you move, so if anything, size up if you're between sizes,” she says.

Material

A vast majority of seamless leggings are made from similar materials, as noted by Lightbown. “Seamless leggings can be made from various materials like nylon, polyester, spandex/elastane, or a blend of these fabrics,” she says. “Look for materials that are breathable, moisture-wicking, and provide stretch and comfort.”

Your Questions, Answered

Are seamless leggings actually fully seamless?

According to Greer, seamless leggings actually aren’t fully seamless. “Seamless leggings just mean that there are minimal seams (often none in the front) and that the leggings are made from one single piece of fabric vs. several pieces seamed together,” she says.

Meanwhile, Rosales notes that “higher quality brands usually have a seam on the inner leg, as both the SKIMS and Set leggings do. Also with a snatched waistband, there's a seam between the leg and the waist of the pant.”

What is the benefit of seamless leggings?

For Lightbown, there are three principal benefits to opting for seamless leggings, the first being how comfortable and well-fitting they are. “Seamless leggings eliminate the presence of bulky or irritating seams, providing a smooth and comfortable fit,” Lightbown says. “This can be especially beneficial during activities that require a wide range of motion, like yoga or running.”

Secondly, this type of leggings allows for more flexibility. “Seamless leggings are designed to stretch and move with your body, offering a greater range of motion,” the expert continues. “This can enhance your overall comfort and performance during physical activities.” And thirdly, Lightbown cites “reduced chafing and irritation” as a clear benefit to leggings of the seamless variety, and we can’t help but concur.

How do you prevent seamless leggings from sliding down?

There are a few things you can do when buying — and wearing — seamless leggings to ensure they won’t slide down. “You want to make sure you have the right size, first and foremost,” says Greer. “Seamless leggings should have a tight fit with no additional fabric or gaping around the waist.” The expert adds, “look for a pair that has a control or compression top listed in the product details and most importantly, follow the wash instructions. Drying seamless leggings can actually make them shrink, which can lead to that annoying roll-down of the waistband.”
