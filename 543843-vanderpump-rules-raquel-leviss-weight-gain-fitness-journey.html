Who is Raquel Leviss?

She is an acclaimed American media personality. Raquel was born Rachel Savannah Leviss on September 12 1994, in Thousand Oaks, California, to Susan Couture and Carey Leviss. She has two elder half-siblings, Kate and David, from her mother's side.

The model went to Newbury Park High School before joining Sonoma State University to study Kinesiology. Later, she graduated from the same school with a Bachelor of Science degree in the same course in 2018.

What does Raquel Leviss do for a living?

Rachel is a multitalented personality. She is a reality TV star, podcast host, model, philanthropist, and social media influencer. She started her modelling career in 2010 but rose to fame after winning the 2016 Miss Sonoma County pageant.

She also participated in the 2020 Miss Malibu/Miss Beverly Hills pageant. Raquel has worked with high-end brands such as:
