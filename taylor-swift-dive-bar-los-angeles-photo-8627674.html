Taylor Swift Channeled &quot;Delicate&quot; at a West Side Dive Bar

Aside from being a newly-minted billionaire, Grammy-winning singer-songwriter Taylor Swift is just a normal girl. And when she's not selling out arenas, Swift enjoys the smaller things in life, like chugging beer and packing on the PDA with her NFL-star boyfriend (Alexa, play that "I think I like this little life" song.)

As if we didn't think Swift could get any more down to Earth, the pop star was spotted enjoying a night out at a local dive bar in West Hollywood. In images obtained by TMZ, Swift can be seen celebrating a friend's birthday at Barney’s Beanery. The group sat at a booth table by the window and shared a pizza, as well as a few other side dishes, according to an onlooker who spoke to People "Taylor was very low-key," one witness told the publication. "She was there for about two hours."

Of course, Swifies will be quick to draw parallels between her recent L.A. outing to lyrics from fan-favorite "Delicate," in which she sings, "Dive bar on the east side, where you at?". The Reputation-era song is believed to be about her once-budding romance with ex Joe Alwyn, and the actual "dive bar on the east side" is reportedly New York City's Lovers of Today in the East Village.

According to TMZ, the megastar's security team covered the tab on her behalf after she left, not an unusual occurrence for the singer. According to Vulture, Swift's security reportedly paid the bill during an outing last year with Laura Dern, Zoë Kravitz, and Greta Gerwig at NYC restaurant Il Buco Alimentari.

Noticeably absent from Thursday's festivities was Swift's man Travis Kelce, though, there's no cause for concern. The two are actually reportedly ready to take the "next steps" and move in together "full-time." A source recently told Us Weekly that the two have discussed bopping back and forth between Los Angeles and New York for part of the year, and then live at Kelce's Kansas City home during the NFL season.

The insider added that the couple is “having so much fun and enjoying things, so there’s no rush [to get engaged]. She loves how down-to-earth Travis is, and it’s a very healthy relationship. Things are very serious, and they’re both thinking of the next steps.”

Another source also told Life & Style that the two are in it for the long haul and committed to each other "for life" while on vacation in the Bahamas. "Travis told Taylor he wanted to spend the rest of his life with her, and she told him she feels the same," they said. "It wasn’t an official proposal, but they’re committed to each other, and they spent the weekend celebrating in paradise.”
