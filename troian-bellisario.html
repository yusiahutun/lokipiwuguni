Troian Bellisario News - Us Weekly

Name:Troian Bellisario

Hometown:Los Angeles, CA

Birthday:October 28, 1985

About

Actress Troian Bellisario was born on October 28, 1985, in L.A. to TV producer Donald P. Bellisario. Growing up she appeared in her father’s shows Quantum Leap and NCIS. She also appeared opposite Mary-Kate and Ashley Olsen in the film Billboard Dad. Bellisario’s big break came years later when she was cast as Spencer Hastings in the ABC Family series Pretty Little Liars. In 2011, Bellisario was nominated for a Teen Choice Award for her role. She is married to actor Patrick J. Adams, whom she met on the set of Equivocation.
