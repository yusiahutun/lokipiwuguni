Hailey Bieber Is the Latest Celeb to Wear Yellow Nails

From the chic bob to the glazed donut nail, Hailey Bieber is a reliable source of beauty inspiration. So it's no surprise that her latest nail look has made its way onto everyone's spring beauty moodboards. Say hello to this season's hottest nail color: yellow nails.

Reminiscent of sunlight and warm weather, yellow nails just radiate joy — all while being a little unexpected. Plus, there are ample ways to make the most of this buttery color, whether you're going for gold or want to bring on the brightness with a highlighter-neon shade. For more of a neutral, on the other hand, it's easy to take it soft and muted. The best part? Yellow is so bold that it can, surprisingly, go with pretty much anything.

But don't just take our word for it. Not only has Bieber been spotted sporting the nail color, but Jennifer Lopez, Selena Gomez, and other stars have turned to the color, too. Here, consider their takes on it — and get inspired for your own riff on the yellow-nail trend for spring.

Hailey Bieber

Created by Bieber's go-to nail artist Zola Ganzorigt, this yellow shade strikes the perfect balance of soft and bright. The rich yellow color doesn't veer into in-your-face blinding territory, however — and makes for the perfect nail look to wear as the weather starts to warm.

Vanessa Hudgens

Forever our queen of cool, Hudgens sported long, bright yellow nails that contrast nicely with her nude lip.

Jennifer Lopez

Leave it to the king of nail trends, Tom Bachik, to serve us the neon-yellow nails of our dreams. Here, Bachik coated Lopez's long, almond-shaped nails with a bright highlighter shade that proves just as eye-catching as her '90s chunky highlights.

Camila Cabello

For a warmer tone, go for something like this golden-mustard shade on Camila Cabello. Also created by Bachik, it works best on her mid-length, almond-shaped nails.

Selena Gomez

Yes, pastel for spring can hardly be called innovation. But if it ain't broke, keep wearing it. Gomez's spin on yellow nails (also created by Bachik) is a muted yellow — ideal if you're looking to add some softness to the color.
