Justin Moore Features Wife and Daughters in New Til My Last Day Video

The video for Justin Moore's new song ''Til My Last Day' is every bit as much the family affair as the song's longing lyrics. During the heartstring-tugger, Moore sings about his dedication to his family, and his wife Kate and daughters Ella Cole and Kennedy Faye are prominently featured throughout the clip.

Many scenes feel like they may have been pulled from the backyard of the Moore family home. Fans get a glimpse at the singer playing with his group on a giant trampoline that sits in a fenced-in yard in a comfortable neighborhood. There are also scenes cut along the beach and in the parking lot of what looks to be a University of Arkansas football game.

''Til My Last Day' is the third single from Moore's 'Outlaws Like Me' album, following 'If Heaven Wasn't So Far Away' and 'Bait a Hook.' Ella Cole is 2-years-old, while Kennedy was just born last November. Justin and Kate have been married since 2007.
