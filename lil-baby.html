Personal Life, Relationship, Current or Past Affairs

Lil Baby’s personal life has gone through many ups and downs. This songwriter has spent a lot of his time in jail. He almost dropped out of tenth grade to become a full-time drug dealer; this was why he had to deal with many arrests in his teenage.

At age 19, Lil Baby was again arrested for a probation violation. Upon his release from jail in 2017, he started his rap career with Perfect Timing, released in April of that year. Lil Baby has two children, one with his former girlfriend, Ayesha and another son with his current girlfriend, Jayda Chaves. Jones is dating  Jayda Cheaves and is the mom of his second son. Jayda is a social media influencer with her brand for weaves, wigs and other hair accessories.

Career

Lil Baby’s debut studio, which features Drake’s album Harder Than Ever (2018), was certified RIAA Platinum, six on the Billboard Hot 100. He released two more mixtapes in 2018, Drip Harder and Street Gossip, containing his most famous song, “Drip Too Hard”, which peaked at number four on the Billboard Hot 100; the latter was second on the US Billboard 200.

His second studio album, My Turn, released in 2020, was number one on the Billboard 200 and was certified three times platinum by the RIAA. In June 2020, he released the single “The Bigger Picture”, also number three on the Hot 100, becoming the highest-charting song as a lead artist of his career. The latest song he released was “Real as it gets”, which was also widely loved by his fans.
