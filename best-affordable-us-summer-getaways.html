Try These Affordable Summer Getaways In The US

Discover Burning Man Art & World’s Largest Free Hot Air Balloon Festival in Reno Tahoe, Nevada. Situated less than 20 minutes from the city's heart, Reno-Tahoe International Airport provides affordable flights to many major U.S. destinations, almost always under $300, to make getting to and from home– a breeze. Travelers can get a taste of the world-renowned Burning Man art for free, as they meander the streets gazing up at the sculptures that cover every corner, showcased in the new Neon Line District. Keep the art vibes going with a free self-guided art tour from ArtSpot and stop into the many unique shops along the route.

Travelers won’t go hungry with the flourishing food scene boasting dozens of local craft breweries in a two-block radius and Food Truck Fridays every summer bringing in over 70 food trucks with affordable dishes in the park. From strolling the Riverwalk District to venturing into the nearby hiking trails, there are plenty of outdoor activities to experience. Visit in September, and check the world’s largest free hot air balloon festival off the bucket list! Stay at The Jesse, a historic Nevada building turned micro-hotel offering just six suites, situated in the walkable Midtown District.

These American getaways offer an enticing range of experiences without breaking the bank. Whether you seek adventure in the great outdoors or vibrant city escapades, you won’t be bored. From the idyllic island of Kaua'i in Hawaii to the surf town of Encinitas in California and the authentic Western experience in Cheyenne, Wyoming, these destinations promise unforgettable moments. Embrace the beauty of Hilton Head in South Carolina, indulge in the vibrant nightlife of Miami or explore the captivating allure of Atlantic City in New Jersey. With these affordable Summer getaways, you can make the most of your vacation while keeping your budget intact.
