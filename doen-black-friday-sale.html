Den's Cyber Monday Sale 2023 Details and 20 Best Deals

Dôen’s Cyber Monday sale—officially known as the “Giving Thanks Sale”—ends tonight, November 27, at midnight. Once again, it’s a place to find heirloom-worthy pieces for a lot less—and for today only, they come with an extra discount.

Everyday outfitting has never felt ordinary at Dôen—a fact I've learned after covering Dôen's every launch and celebrity placement for the past three years. This label specializes in dresses with hand-painted prints and dreamy silhouettes with comfortable smocking and adjustable straps. It’s whimsical yet practical fare that can make commuting to work feel like starring in a modernized Austen novel. Naturally, fall dresses, like the smocked Bijou dress, are the highlight of Dôen’s Cyber Monday sale. There are bucolic midi and maxi floral dresses for nearly half-off, as well as luscious silk dresses for less than $200. Long sleeves and dramatic skirts are fit for the upcoming holiday party circuit, while past-season summer maxi dresses and flirty minis are worth buying now to save for next season. These on-sale dresses are designed for romanticizing your life, no matter which cut speaks to you.

Dôen gives a rustic-chic update to so much more than dresses (pillowy cardigan sweaters, English countryside herringbone coats, full fall skirts), and every category Dôen designs is included in the brand’s Cyber Monday sale. Knitwear ranges from crewneck sweaters with sweet side-button details to pointelle T-shirts made for layering. Denim leans wide-leg and structured like a trouser—the sort of pair Jane Birkin would approve of. Dôen’s artful accessories are also a must-shop—I tracked down a gorgeous pair of suede pointed ballet flats and a black leather belt with a sculptural buckle in limited quantities.

Dôen’s Cyber Monday sale ends tonight, November 27, at midnight, and all pieces are an extra 20 percent off at checkout. Below, I’ve curated my 16 favorite deals included in the current batch: cuddly striped sweaters, stately wool coats, structured trousers, and of course, whimsical floral dresses. Some pieces are already flying off shelves, so it's worth taking a good look now. Each one will make an everyday fall or winter outfit feel special—if you order before it sells out.
