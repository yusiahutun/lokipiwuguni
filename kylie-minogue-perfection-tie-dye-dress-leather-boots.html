Kylie Minogue is perfection in funky tie-dye dress and knee-high leather boots

The Aussie Padam Padam hitmaker looked gorgeous in green

    Kylie Minogue resembled a bodacious bombshell on Wednesday as she stepped out for a glitzy event with her eponymous wine brand.

    In a snapshot shared to Instagram Stories, the songstress, 55, looked picture-perfect in a funky navy and forest green tie-dye midi dress complete with a small thigh-split, a fringed hem and a single long sleeve.

    Kylie is no stranger to a leather look, check out her figure-flattering dress in the video below...

    Kylie completed her glamorous get-up with a pair of sassy knee-high leather boots, a bewitching black manicure and two simple silver rings.

    The 'Padam Padam' singer wore her platinum blonde locks in cascading waves around her shoulders and finished off her look with a touch of pretty peach-pink makeup.

    In the clip, the blonde beauty appeared in high spirits as she relaxed on a tan leather sofa whilst residing over a bowl of chilled sparkling rose nestled amongst pink and purple flowers.

    Kylie launched her eponymous wine brand in 2020 with the help of London-based distributor, Benchmark Drinks. Since its launch, the star's brand has gone on to win multiple prestigious awards. The Kylie Minogue Rosé is an elegant, bespoke blend made from Carignan and Cabernet Sauvignon grapes sourced from the sun-drenched southern French Coast.

    And back in October 2022, the star celebrated the launch of a brand-new wine – an alcohol-free sparkling rose.

    This isn't the first time Kylie has turned heads in the spotlight. On Monday, the star whipped fans into a frenzy when she debuted a sheer, lime green dress.

    Never one to shy away from a bold look, Kylie wore the striking garment for a new photo shoot in a plush hotel room. She toughened up her look with a pair of snug leather boots and wore her Rapunzel locks in mermaid-esque waves.

    Awestruck fans and friends raced to weigh in on Kylie's fashion update, with one writing: "Gorgeous dress, always so stylish," while another chimed in: "Love this neon green dress."

    A third remarked: "You always look amazing in boots!" and a fourth enthused: "I'm green with envy!!!" followed by a pair of green heart emojis.

    It's been a busy time for the beloved pop sensation. Back in July, Kylie confirmed her upcoming Las Vegas residency at the Venetian Resort.

    Kylie's first-ever U.S. residency kicks off on 3 November, and will also serve as opening night for the Venetian's brand-new entertainment venue, Voltaire. The intimate, 1,000-seat space aims to host not just superstars like Kylie but DJs as well as cabaret and burlesque acts too.

    "I always thought [a residency] would happen at some point. So, now is the right time to be in the Voltaire environment," Kylie told

    "I think if I had done this earlier in my career it might have been too early. If I'd done it too much later, I'd regret it being too much later. Right now, in my life, in my career, it feels like the perfect point, the perfect moment where I've got the catalogue, I've got the history, but I've also got the drive and the ambition to go so much further."
