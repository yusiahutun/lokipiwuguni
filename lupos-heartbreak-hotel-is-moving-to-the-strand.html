Lupos Heartbreak Hotel Is Moving To The Strand

PROVIDENCE (CelebrityAccess News Service) – Effective December 8, 2004 Lupo's will be moving – 2 blocks – to The Strand, 79 Washington Street, downtown Providence, Rhode Island.

They'll now be know as LUPO'S AT THE STRAND.

The Strand is a beautifully restored art deco theater. The capacity of the first floor is 1,200, and they are renovating the balcony which will open in February with a capacity of 750.

LUPO'S AT THE STRAND – opening line-up includes:

December 8
CHEVELLE
with FINGER 11 and acoustic COLD

December 9
SEAL
with WILSHIRE

December 11
EVE 6
with CAMPFIRE GIRLS & CAUTERIZE

December 27
THE SLIP

Unfortunately ‘The Met,’ the small 230 seat club, owned by, and located at the old Lupo’s location, will be closing, but they will continue to promote smaller shows at:
ULTRA LIVE – capacity 500
THE CALL – capacity 300
and CENTURY LOUNGE – capacity 200

Lupo’s continues to be booked exclusively by Jack Reich at Heartbreak Hits.
