Austin’s biggest night in country music is fast approaching. If you’re a fan of the genre, you won’t want to miss it—which is exactly why we’re sharing how to watch the CMT Music Awards live for free, so you can get to see which of your favorite artists take home a trophy.

The CMT Music Awards, an annual ceremony celebrating the best in country music videos, has been a staple in the industry since its inception in 1967. Back then, the ceremony was known as the Music City News Awards, and has gone through a few name changes in the decades since before officially becoming the version of the ceremony we know today. Originally held in Nashville, Tennessee, the awards show has since moved to Austin, Texas and continues to grow in popularity, attracting millions of viewers each year.

For country music enthusiasts, the CMT Music Awards stands out because it happens to be one of the only awards shows in the genre where winners are voted for by fans. So, whether you’re rooting for established stars like Carrie Underwood and Keith Urban or cheering on rising talents like Zach Bryan and Ashley Cooke, you’ll know that every winning artist is a fan favorite.

In recent years, streaming options have made it easier than ever to watch the CMT Music Awards live online for free. So, whether you’re a die-hard fan or simply curious to see what this year’s show has in store, don’t miss your chance to find out how to watch the CMT Music Awards live for free below.     • Where are the 2024 CMT Music Awards taking place?

    The 2024 CMT Music Awards will take place at the Moody Center in Austin, Texas. In the past, the CMT Music Awards were hosted in Nashville, Tennessee—but the ceremony was moved to Austin for the first time in 2023. “The CMT Music Awards are coming back to Austin in 2024, and we couldn’t be more excited. As the ‘Live Music Capital of the World,’ Austin is no stranger to celebrating its vibrant music community and showcasing an eclectic array of musical talents,” Austin Mayor Kirk Watson said in a statement. “We’re proud that the CMT Music Awards are returning to Austin, and we can’t wait to welcome artists and visitors to our city.”

    Who is the CMT Music Awards 2024 host?

    For the fourth year in a row, Kelsea Ballerini will act as host for the show. This year marks the first time Ballerini will host the ceremony solo—in previous years, she has co-hosted with stars like Kane Brown and Anthony Mackie. “I couldn’t be more excited to be back for my fourth year with my CMT family to host this year’s CMT Music Awards in Austin, Texas,” Ballerini said in a statement. “Hosting an awards show that celebrates the music that the fans love most has been an honor, and I know this year will be another unforgettable night.”
