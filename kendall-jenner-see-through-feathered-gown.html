See Kendall Jenner Look Stunning in a See-Through Feathered Gown

Kendall Jenner loves to make a statement in striking sheer looks.

Last night, the supermodel attended the retrospective for Karl Lagerfeld at luxury vintage store What Goes Around Comes Around in NYC. The style star looked stunning in a sleeveless couture gown by Alexandre Vauthier, comprised of a see-through, mock turtleneck bodice and a lavish feathered skirt with faux-fur embellishments.

The 818 founder completed the ensemble with pointed-toe satin boots in a matching shade of royal blue, also from the French luxury house. Her accessories included a pair of small stud earrings and a blue crocodile print clutch in one hand. As for her beauty look, the model's hair was pulled into a neat ponytail, and she paired her sun-kissed make-up look with a pink lip.

BACKGRID

Jenner shared several pics of the fun look on Instagram, starting the carousel with a pic where she smiles widely as she exists a black SUV to pose under the red umbrella held by her chauffeur. She also documented the ensemble with a mirror selfie.

The model has been embracing fashion's renewed affinity for all things sheer over the past season. Earlier this month, she put her own spin on the naked dress trend in a white bodycon midi dress from Alaïa which featured an embroidered skeleton-esque structure and see-through mesh panels between the skeletal embellishments.

She also wore a statement-making monochromatic outfit during a trip to Paris last month, which included a gray wool dress by Ann Demeulemeester. The long-sleeved dress featured an asymmetrical thigh-high slit, and a mock neck with an attached full-length scarf adornment over the right shoulder. She finished the look with knee-high pointed-toe leather boots by The Row, a spacious black leather tote bag, and black rectangular sunglasses.
