About

Corey Fogelmanis is a 24 year old American Actor. Born Corey Shain Fogelmanis on 13th August, 1999 in Thousand Oaks, California, U.S., he is famous for Girl Meets World. His zodiac sign is Leo.

Sabrina Carpenter is a 24 year old American Singer. Born Sabrina Annlynn Carpenter on 11th May, 1999 in Quakertown, Pennsylvania, USA, she is famous for her roles as Maya Hart in the Disney Channel comedy series Girl Meets World, Jenny Parker in the 2016 Disney Channel made-for-TV adventure comedy film Adventures in Babysitting, Melissa Chase (voice) in the Disney Channel animated comedy series Milo Murphy's Law, Hailey in the 2018 drama film The Hate U Give, Harper Kreyman in the 2019 Netflix teen romantic comedy film Tall Girl and its 2022 sequel film Tall Girl 2 and Quinn Ackerman in the 2020 Netflix dance comedy film Work It, in addition to her music career consisting of albums, EPs, and tracks like Evolution, Eyes Wide Open, Can’t Blame a Girl for Trying, Thumbs, Why, On My Way (with Alan Walker and Farruko), The Middle of Starting Over, and We’ll Be the Stars, and her album Emails I Can't Send in a career that spans 2011–present. Her zodiac sign is Taurus.
