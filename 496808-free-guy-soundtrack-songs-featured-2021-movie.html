Free Guy soundtrack: All songs featured in the 2021 movie

Free Guy is a 2021 action and comedy film starring Ryan Reynolds, Jodie Comer, Joe Keery, and Dwayne Johnson. Like any film, it has soundtracks in almost every scene. The selection of music in the movie was excellent and suitable for a movie of this kind. The music ranges from famous artists to budding artists and even classic film soundtracks. Free Guy soundtracks are unique and make the viewing more entertaining.

Free Guy is a 1 hour and 55 minutes long movie, and nearly every scene contains some music. The characters' drama, conversations, witty lines, scenes, and music tell it all about the film. The film has a rating of 7.1/10 on IMDb and an 80% rating on Rotten Tomatoes.
