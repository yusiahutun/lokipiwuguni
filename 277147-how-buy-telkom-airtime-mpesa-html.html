How to buy Telkom airtime through M-Pesa (in a few steps)

Do you know how to buy Telkom airtime through M-Pesa? Telkom is one of the telecommunication networks with thousands of users due to its awesome offers, from free calls to amazing data bundle packages. However, most people have had issues purchasing Telkom airtime with Mpesa. If you're sailing in the same boat, that should no longer be your worry.

Can I buy Telkom airtime using M-Pesa? Yes, you can. Buying Telkom airtime through M-Pesa is a service that is facilitated by Telkom and other service providers such as Pesapal and Ipay Limited.

How to buy Telkom airtime through M-Pesa

It is possible to top up Telkom airtime from your M-Pesa. Therefore, you do not have to walk to the shop to purchase the airtime if you have cash in your Mpesa wallet.

You can use the Telkom paybill number or other options, such as the Pesapal Paybill and Ipay Limited numbers. Interestingly, many Telkom users prefer to use Ipay Limited because it gives them a great 100% bonus on every top-up.

The step-by-step procedures on how to buy Telkom airtime from Mpesa using Telkom paybill are easy. Follow these steps to pay Telkom airtime via M-Pesa.

  • Go to the M-Pesa menu.
  • Select Pay Bill alternative under Lipa na M-Pesa.
  • Enter the Telkom paybill no 777711.
  • Enter the account, which is your Telkom mobile number.
  • Key in the amount that you want to spend.
  • Enter your M-pesa PIN, then send.
  • Wait for M-pesa confirmation, followed by the airtime.

How to top up someone using Telkom

Here is the procedure you should use to top up Telkom line using M-Pesa line using the T-kash app.

  • Dial *160# or Use the T-kash app.
  • Select “Buy Airtime”.
  • Select “Buy My Phone” or “Other Phone”.
  • Enter ”Telkom Number”.
  • Enter “Amount”.
  • Confirm the transaction.
  • Enter T-kash PIN
  • You will receive a confirmation SMS from T-kash

    How to buy Telkom airtime using Mpesa from third parties

    Other service providers like Pesapal, Ipay Limited, and Jazalink offer this service. The steps are the same as above, although the Pay Bill number differs. Below are some of the procedures you can use to top up Telkom line using M-Pesa.

  • Go to the Mpesa menu.
  • Select the Lipa na Mpesa option.
  • Click on the Pay Bill option.
  • Enter the Paybill number for (Pesapal - 220220, Ipay Limited - 510800, and Jazalink - 309028)
  • Enter the account number, TELKXXXXXX, if using Pesapal. In this case, XXXXXX is your Telkom mobile number. For other service providers, you do not need to start with TELK. Simply enter your Telkom number as the account number.
  • Enter the amount of credit you need.
  • Enter your Mpesa secret PIN.

    • Remember that you should not disclose your M-Pesa PIN to anyone. Enter this secret number correctly and wait patiently for a confirmation text of your successful transaction.

    Example:

    If you require a Ksh. 100 airtime, your Telkom phone number is 07728999**, and your pin is 0000. All you have to do is to enter the Pay Bill business number 220220 and enter TELK07728999** as the account number.

    Enter 100 as the amount, and finally, enter 0000 as your secret M-Pesa PIN. Remember that Ksh. 22 fees will be deducted from your M-Pesa account for the service because you need credit above Ksh. 100.

    You will receive a confirmation message from M-Pesa of the transaction, then wait patiently for the credit. Sometimes the process takes longer if the system is busy, but not more than 10 minutes. Always confirm your Telkom phone number before entering it to avoid buying credit for another user.
