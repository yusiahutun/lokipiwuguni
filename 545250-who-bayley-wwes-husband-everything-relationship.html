Who is Bayley?

She is an American professional wrestler whose real name is Pamela Rose Martinez. She was born in the Bay Area suburb of Newark, California, on June 15, 1989, to Thomas Martinez and Annmarie Martinez. Her father is a Mexican-American, while her mother is an Anglo-American.

Thomas, her dad, was a professional wrestler in independent circuits who went by the ring name Speedy. Her mother, Annmarie, worked in the same industry as a ring announcer for Big Time Wrestling and a ticket salesperson.

She has two sisters and one brother. Little information is known about Bayley's siblings as she has not given reports about them on public domains. Bayley grew up with her siblings in San José, California. She attended Independence High School and was a standout student because of her interest in sports.

As a child, she enjoyed watching WWE films. But her mother would not allow her to because, at that time, wrestlers used to have pillow fight matches. She began watching WWE films regularly only after her parents divorced, becoming a fan of wrestlers such as Ivory and Lita.

Career

At the age of ten, Bayley developed an interest in wrestling. In 2008, she joined Big Time Wrestling's training classes with Jason Styles as her trainer. She had her first match in September, using the ring name Davina Rose.

Bayley continued training until 2012 while wrestling for promotions like Shimmer Women Athletes, NWA Championship Wrestling, and Shine Wrestling. In March 2012, Bayley won her first match in Shimmer Women Athletes Volume 48.

By December, she had signed a contract with World Wrestling Entertainment (WWE). She debuted on WWE NXT in January 2013 under the ring name Bayley. Her first win at the WWE was in September 2013.

Despite her initial win, Bayley faced several losses until January 2014. In August 2014, she became the number 1 contender for the NXT Women's Championship but lost the match. However, in August 2015, she won her first NXT Women's Championship.

Bayley debuted on WWE's main roster in July 2016 at Battleground. In February 2017, she won the WWE Raw Women's Championship but lost it in April. She then had a personal feud with Sasha Banks, which resulted in the formation of The Boss 'n' Hug Connection in July 2018.

In February 2019, according to Forbes, Bayley and Banks won the inaugural WWE Women's Tag Team Championship. However, they lost the title in March.

In May 2019, Bayley won the women's Money in the Bank ladder match and used her contracts to triumph in the SmackDown Women's Championship that night, making her the first WWE Women's Grand Slam winner in history. In 2024, she won the Royal Rumble match and set a new women's record of 1 hour and 3 minutes.
