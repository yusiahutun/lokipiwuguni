Bethenny Frankels Sweetest Mother-Daughter Photos With Bryn

Bethenny Frankel has a special bond with her and ex Jason Hoppy’s daughter, Bryn, often sharing their mother-daughter milestones.

The Real Housewives of New York City alum gave birth to her daughter in May 2010, two years before she and Hoppy split. 

After years of a tumultuous custody battle, Frankel was awarded full legal custody of Bryn in 2020. One year later, a judge ruled that the reality TV star no longer needed to pay Hoppy child support.

With the legal drama settled, the Skinnygirl founder has remained focused on raising Bryn.
