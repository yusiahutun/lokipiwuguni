Taylor Swift Looks Like A Different Person On The Cover of Wonderland Magazine

Is that you, Taylor Swift?!

The "Blank Space" singer covers the latest issue of Wonderland magazine, where she takes on a strikingly different new look.

Rocking greasy slicked back hair, bold brows and natural makeup, the 24-year-old looks gorgeous yet unrecognizable in the cover photo.

Thomas Whiteside shot Swift for the new issue of Wonderland, and toned down the singer's look by trading in her signature cat eye liner and red lipstick for an earth-toned smokey eye and a matching sheer gloss.

The "Out of the Woods" songstress is also far more covered up, wearing a chunky white sweater instead of her normal crop tops and A-line dresses.
