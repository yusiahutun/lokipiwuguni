See All 3 of Jennifer Lopez's Jaw-Dropping Bridal Looks

Jennifer Lopez celebrated her union with husband Ben Affleck with three utterly jaw-dropping bridal looks custom-made by Ralph Lauren and more than $2 million worth of jewelry.

Lopez and Affleck, who previously married in a surprise Las Vegas chapel ceremony back in July, said their "I dos" for a second time in front of family and friends at Affleck's lavish estate in Riceboro, Georgia, this past weekend.

Though paparazzi photos previously captured Lopez's dramatic veil and train for the special day, a new video released by Ralph Lauren and a new edition of her fan newsletter, On the JLo, offer an inside look at the making of her three glamorous bridal gowns.

To walk down the aisle, J.Lo dazzled in a turtleneck column dress featuring dramatic, voluminous ruffles halfway down the skirt. The flared cape sleeves, open mesh back, and cathedral veil made for a showstopping appearance. Her jewelry only amplified that effect; she opted for $92,000 worth in pearl and diamond pieces set in platinum from luxe Japanese jeweler Mikimoto, including earrings and a floral ring.

Meanwhile, Affleck also dressed in Ralph Lauren, looking suave in a white tuxedo jacket, black trousers, and a black bow tie.

Later in the night, J.Lo changed into two other wedding gowns from Ralph Lauren. One channeled Old Hollywood with its body-hugging silhouette and head-to-toe stringed pearl details. The other embraced a more modern and sleek design, with a keyhole cutout on the chest, twisted back straps, and embedded Swarovski crystals.

OnTheJLo.com

Accessorizing her pearl gown, J.Lo wore another pair of earrings from Mikimoto, this set costing $85,000. For her reception, she swapped the pearls for 27-carat one-of-a-kind pear-shape drop flower marquise diamond earrings from Samer Halimeh, which alone cost a whopping $2 million.

OnTheJLo.com

For part of their Vegas nuptials, Lopez wore the same white dress she appeared in on the poster of Jersey Girl, the 2004 flick she starred in with Affleck.

The piece hails from Alexander McQueen and features textured floral jacquard fabric, a jewel neckline, a corseted bodice, and a gathered full skirt.

"I've had this dress for so many years, and I've just been saving it, saving it, saving it, and now I'm wearing it on my wedding day," Lopez later said of her wedding outfit in a video shared in her On the JLo newsletter.
