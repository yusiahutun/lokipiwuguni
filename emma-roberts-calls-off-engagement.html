Emma Roberts and Evan Peters reportedly end engagement

Wedding bells won't soon be ringing for American Horror Story stars Emma Roberts and Evan Peters, according to new reports. The couple, who have been together since 2012, are said to have called off their engagement over a year after they announced their intention to tie the knot."Their three years together just ran its course," Just Jared reported a source as saying on Friday. "They have so many great memories together but couldn't make it work in the end."The breakup was amicable," the insider added. "There was no drama whatsoever. Emma and Evan are even still friends!"
