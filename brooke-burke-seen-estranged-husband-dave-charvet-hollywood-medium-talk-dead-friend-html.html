Brooke Burke with estranged husband Dave Charvet on Hollywood Medium

Brooke Burke announced weeks ago she was divorcing husband of seven years Dave Charvet.

So it was a surprise to see him on a teaser for next week's episode of Hollywood Medium.

The Playboy cover girl was on Tyler Henry's show to ask about the passing of her late friend and former hairstylist Steven Lake, who died in 2013 during an accident.
