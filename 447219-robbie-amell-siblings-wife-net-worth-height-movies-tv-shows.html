Early life

The actor was born on April 21, 1988, in Toronto, United States of America. Amell’s full name is Robert Patrick Amell IV. His mother is Jennifer (née Burden), while his father is Robert Patrick "Rob" Amell III. He was brought up together with his sister.

Robbie Amell’s family belongs to the Jewish religion. He attended Armour Heights Public School for his basic education and graduated in 2000. For the love of acting, Amell enrolled at Canadian Studios Acting Academy. Later he graduated from Lawrence Park Collegiate Institute in Toronto in 2006.

Career

With his sister, Robbie Amell started to land modelling and acting in minor roles in commercials when he was six years old. However, at the age of sixteen, he began landing roles in high school plays such as Louis and Dave and Fionia, Picasso at the Lapin Agile, and The Importance of Being Earnest.
