Early Life and Childhood

Michael Wayans was born on March 28, 1985, and he is currently 36 years old. He hails from Los Angeles, California, and his zodiac sign is Aries. Likewise, his full name is Michael Richard Wayans. Talking about his family, his parents are his father Damon Wayans, and his mother Lisa Thorner.

Similarly, his siblings are Damon Wayans Jr., Cara Mia Wayans, and Kyla Wayans. Damon Jr. is his only elder sibling born in 1982. Likewise, his younger siblings – Cara and Kyla – were born in 1987 and in 1991 respectively.

Talking more about his extended family, he is the nephew of Dwayne Wayans, Keenen Ivory Wayans, Kim Wayans, Nadia Wayans, Marlon Wayans, Shawn Wayans, Elvira Wayans, Diedre Wayans, and Vonnie Wayans. Most of his siblings, aunts, and uncles are famous actors and writers. However, there is no verified information regarding his educational journey.

Career, Professional Life, and Net Worth

Professionally, Michael Wayans is a famous actor and composer. As an actor, he gained fame for his prominent roles in Blankman released in 1994, Dance Flick released in 2009, My Wife and Kids released in 2001, and Blöd-Man.

Moreover, he starred in a movie for the first time when he was only 9 years old. Later on, the actor began his professional acting career in the year 1994. This was with his appearance in the film Blankman. This was his father’s 1994 film.

For Dance Flick, he worked alongside several of his family members including Marlon Wayans, Shawn Wayans, and Damon Wayans. It is a 2009 satirical comedy. Then from the year 2001 to 2003, he played the role of Mike on the hit series, My Wife and Kids.

Further, he has also appeared on the HBO comedy special One Night Stand. Moving on, he has a net worth estimation of approximately $2.5 million US dollars.
