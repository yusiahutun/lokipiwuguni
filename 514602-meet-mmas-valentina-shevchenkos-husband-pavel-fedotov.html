Interesting facts about Valentina Shevchenko

Pavel Fedotov has played a significant role in the success of Valentina Shevchenko as an MMA fighter. He has trained Valentina since she was young and continues to do an impressive job today. Here are some interesting facts about her:

For many years, people have thought Pavel Fedotov was Valentina Shevchenko's partner. However, Pavel and Valentina share no romantic relationship. The two have never been married or dated. Instead, they have been in a professional setting for many years.

Pavel has been Antonina’s trainer since the early 2000s. He has helped her win many MMA, UFC, and Muay Thai bouts. Many have claimed that Pavel Fedotov’s wife is Antonina Shevchenko, Valentina’s sister.

Pavel and Valentina’s sister have not openly claimed to be dating. Also, Pavel has been a father figure to the two sisters for a long time. The three individuals are private and never share their personal lives.

3. She worked with Pavel and Antonina as martial arts teachers

Valentina Shevchenko and Pavel Fedotov have shared a professional relationship for over 20 years. The two met in 2000 when Valentina was 12 years old. Valentina was in a fight with a 22-year-old opponent. She knocked out her opponent, despite being ten years younger.

They worked as mixed martial arts teachers in Peru and participated in bouts. Eventually, the three officially became Peru citizens.
