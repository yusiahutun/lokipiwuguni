Anne Hathaway Brings the Shimmer in a Sequin-and-Crystal Jumpsuit

Who says sparkly party fits are just for nighttime? Anne Hathaway certainly proved otherwise Sunday, when she arrived at the 39th Film Independent Spirit Awards in a jumpsuit that shimmered like a disco ball.

Hathaway walked the red carpet at the Santa Monica event in the magnificent sequin- and crystal-embellished piece by Valentino: a halterneck jumpsuit covered entirely in milky-white sequins and accented with clear crystals.

Tiny crystals lined the neckline and created a symmetrical stripe design along the bodice, ending at the waist. A rain of evenly scattered crystals adorned the wide-leg pants, which swished as Hathaway walked.

Amanda Edwards//Getty Images

Styled by Erin Walsh, the actor wore the dazzling party piece with some silver platform sandals by Aquazzura, white diamond drop earrings and rings by Bulgari, and elongated oval sunglasses in a foggy gray by Versace. Her manicure, by Tom Bachik, was a similar smooth blueish gray.

Hathaway kept her makeup look natural and wore her hair in easy waves and wispy bangs, letting her outfit do the talking.

Jeff Kravitz//Getty Images

The Eileen star had a busy weekend, full of glamorous appearances. On Friday, she made an appearance at Milan Fashion Week for Versace’s Fall/Winter 2024 show, looking incredible in a fiery-red leather corset dress pulled straight from the runway. Hours later, Hathaway celebrated fashion in a checkered co-ord at a Fashion Week after-party, where she was spotted showing off her best dance moves.

Somehow, she then also made it to the SAG Awards on Saturday, where she wore a cerulean gown to reunite with The Devil Wears Prada costars Meryl Streep and Emily Blunt, and marking the film’s 18th anniversary.
