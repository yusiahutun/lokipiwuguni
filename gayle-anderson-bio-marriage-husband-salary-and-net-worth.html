Gayle Anderson Bio, Marriage, Husband, Salary and Net Worth

Young Black Achievers Award-winning reporter Gayle Anderson has been actively working in the journalism field since the early 80s. Being one of the top reporters in the United States, Anderson has received several prestigious awards to her name.

Gayle is an award-winning American journalist working as a morning news reporter, anchor, and producer at KTLA Channel 5 News. She rose to fame for her reporting on unique and exciting stories in Southern California.

Career Overview

Gayle started her career in the early 1980s as a National Black Network and ABC-TV field producer. She has served for radio stations, including ABC, NBC, WDRC-AM, WKND-AM, and WWCO-AM.
