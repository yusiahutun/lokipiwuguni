Cooper's Dream Career took a Turn: Know about his Injury

Manning was also good at basketball. His team won a state championship back in 1993. During that time, Cooper felt he was having some problem with his right hand while playing basketball, which he ignored, the thinking was a minor injury.

Sounds like a movie, but later, when the symptoms got serious, he was diagnosed with a congenital condition known as Spinal Stenosis, which narrows the spinal canal. His dream then shattered. He started playing football when he was in fifth grade. 

He mentioned that

It changes your whole world. I’d played organized football since fifth grade. And here I was at school, making friends, living in an athletic dorm, and all of a sudden, they tell you you can’t do that anymore. I had to struggle with it for a while. Yeah, I guess I played my entire career a hit away from the wheelchair; thank God I was kind of a wuss and ran out of bounds so many times.
