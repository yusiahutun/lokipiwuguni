Margot Robbie's Suicide Squad tour beauty looks are on point

    Margot Robbie has firmly established herself as a beauty icon as she tours the world promoting her latest film . 

    The Australian beauty has been turning heads with her bold beauty looks, from boho chic braided hairstyles to bold lipstick shades – and we've rounded up her best looks to date…   

    Showing off her vampy side with an eye-catching dark berry-hued lipstick, adding a touch of laidback chic with a low, messy bun, leaving strands loose to frame her face. 

    Mastering daytime glamour by opting for barely-there make-up save for a slick of statement red lipstick. 

    Giving us summer beauty inspiration with pink lipstick and a hint of highlighting to enhance her radiant complexion – and we're loving her gorgeous tousled ponytail with twist detailing. 

    Paying tribute to her character Harley Quinn's hairstyle by wearing her hair in pigtails, but opting for a low style in loose cascading curls. 

    Opting for pretty pink tones with shimmering rose-hued eyeshadow and matte pink lips, paired with long voluminous lashes, keeping her hair off her face with a chic plaited half updo. 

    Mastering the braid trend with a boho chic side plait, teamed with shimmering pink lipstick and a hint of eyeliner. 

    Looking effortlessly cool for an appearance on with bold smokey eye make-up and loose tousled wavy hair. 

    Showing off her radiant complexion with barely-there make-up, and giving us hair goals with her natural wavy locks worn down with a middle parting for a day at Comic Con.
