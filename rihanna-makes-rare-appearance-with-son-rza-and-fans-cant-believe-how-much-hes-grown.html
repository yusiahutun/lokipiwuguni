Rihanna makes rare appearance with son RZA and fans can't believe how much he's grown

Rihanna has been spotted on a rare public outing with her eldest son, RZA, and fans can’t get over how much he’s grown.

The music star has two sons with her partner, A$AP Rocky, RZA, and Riot Rose. It looks like the one-year-old has taken after his mom and his dad as he was spotted looking ultra stylish as he headed on a playdate with his mom, Rihanna.

Rihanna steps out on playdate with adorable eldest son, RZA

The mom and son stepped out in Los Angeles as they enjoyed a day out at BallnBounce Kids Playhouse, Daily Mail reports.

The Diamonds singer showed off her new short blonde hair as she looked chic in a stylish leopard print coat over a Metallica T-Shirt as she held her son. She topped off the effortless look with a pair of black shades.

RZA, who fans say is his ‘father’s twin,’ was also stepping out with the swag, as he wore a denim jacket over a checkered hoodie.

As the adorable pics spread around the internet, fans took to X, formerly known as Twitter to comment on the adorable duo, and some couldn’t believe how grown up RZA is now!

“OMG Our nephew is growing up y’all,” penned one shocked fan.

“Such a glamourous mother,” another wrote.

A third excited fan penned: “Can’t wait to see pics of RZA and Riot playing together.”

“He’s his brother twin Rihanna had the same baby 2 times,” a fourth laughed.

The star has her ‘fingers crossed’ for baby number 3

The biggest question on everyone’s mind is when Rihanna will drop a new album. However, it looks like we’re not getting an answer to that any time soon.

So, the second question is, is she planning baby number 3 anytime soon? Well, the star may have hinted at it.

At the 36-year-old’s Fenty x Puma Launch Party in December E! News host Justin Sylvester asked if there’s anything the singer doesn’t do. “So far, have daughters!” she joked.

“I’m batting at 75 percent for a boy next time,” she admitted. “So, we’ll just keep our fingers crossed.”
