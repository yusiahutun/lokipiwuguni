Draya Michele Drops $40k to Redecorate Her Mint Swim Offices -- See Major Makeover!

Former "Basketball Wives LA" star Draya Michele doesn't mind dropping some serious cash when it comes to decorating her company warehouse.

Sources tell toofab Draya hired artist Tonia Calderon -- who recently created Blac Chyna's art piece gifted from Rob Kardashian -- to pimp out her Mint Swim space, which includes a warehouse, two offices and a bathroom.

Draya's had the company for several years -- and made major headlines when Selena Gomez wore one of her swimsuits in 2015 -- and wanted to decorate and remodel her Pasadena space so she could hold more meetings and photo shoots on site.

We're told during the month long project, Tonia brought in new furniture, remodeled the bathroom, refinished furniture and created two murals -- which cost $4k each.
