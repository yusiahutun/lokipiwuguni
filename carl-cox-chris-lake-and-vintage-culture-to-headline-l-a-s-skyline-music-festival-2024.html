Carl Cox, Chris Lake, And Vintage Culture To Headline L.A.s Skyline Music Festival 2024

LOS ANGELES, CA (CelebrityAccess) — Insomniac‘s Skyline Music Festival returns to Los Angeles for the third year with a lineup featuring some of the biggest names in electronic music.

Set for February 24 & 25 at L.A.’s Gloria Molina Grand Park in downtown Los Angeles, the festival will feature two stages of music headlined by artists such as techno legend Carl Cox, tech house innovator Chris Lake, and house music icons Vintage Culture.

The lineup for 2024 also includes Marco Carola, Klangkuenstler, Deborah de Luca, Indira Paganotto, Nico Moreno, Adiel, and Palma, among others.

Tickets for Skyline LA go on sale this Friday, January 12 at SkylineFest.com.
