The first thing that pops into your mind, when you see a beautiful and talented celebrity like Kristen Ledlow is " Is she dating?"

Well, Kristen was very secretive about her personal life and least regarding her personal life has circulated in the media. But she was romancing with her boyfriend secretly, without the knowledge of the public.

Through a tweet on September 12, 2015, Kristen revealed that she once had a boyfriend, who played baseball at Jacksonville Also, she added that her then-partner had dumped her through a letter he left on her counter. 

Besides being so popular among boys, her dating life remained shallow or at least that is what she is tried to show. But, that did not stop her followers from wanting to be that lucky guy by her side. 

However, she crushed all hearts after her secretive dating life along with details of her getting married surfaced out among her fans.

Kristen Ledlow's Love!

Apart from her love to husband and sports, Kristen is the proud owner of her cat named 'Magic.' She spoke about how she found Magic and the story behind his name to catster.com on September 15, 2016,

“On the day I adopted Magic, he was the last kitten in the very back and had his paws reaching through his kennel’s door,”

Also, Kristen shared Magic's tragic story in which Magic's mother abandoned him. So, Magic was deprived of learning cat-like behavior from his mother.

“At the shelter, I was told by a volunteer that he had been abandoned and hadn’t spent enough time with his mother to learn cat-like behaviors so that he may pick up some of my ‘human behaviors’ instead. Within a couple of weeks, he was opening the kitchen cabinet to get food, turning on the sink to get water, and even sleeping under the covers.”

Kristen loves her cat and timely flaunts him through both her Twitter and Instagram handle. 

Interestingly, she never misses to wish him on every National Cat Day. Though not kid, Kristen indeed has a baby cat to parent with her husband as a family.
