Alamour the Label Has the Trendiest Formal Dresses For Under $200

If I were stranded on a desert island and had to choose a piece of clothing to wear, it would be a formal dress. Ok, maybe it’s not a very practical choice. But seriously, formal dresses are the underrated wardrobe workhorses. A good formal dress can be for every single special occasion with just a simple swap of a few key accessories. And with fall around the corner, we girls have to get ready for the onslaught of weddings, holidays, and cozy dinner dates to come, as well as once-in-a-lifetime nights like homecoming.

Gone are the days when you had to step foot in a dilapidated mall to stock up on frocks. There are so many great options for dresses that can be shipped directly to your door, especially the ones from Alamour the Label. Any dress you can dream up, they have, with details like cutouts, crystal embellishments, corset boning, rosettes, and more. No matter your personal style or budget, you are sure to find a dress that speaks to you. And bonus! Chances are, no one else will have this look because you didn’t just get it from anywhere. You got it from a super hot Australian brand that’s known for its supermodel clientele.
