Kate Middletons Manolo Blahnik Hangisi shoes: Where to shop the iconic look

Kate Middleton wore a pair of Manolo Blahnik Hangisi pumps (yes, the style made famous by Carrie Bradshaw) in the portrait of herself and Prince William - and everyone is still talking about the iconic footwear brand that's known for elegant heeled frames and delicately embellished detailing.

From where to buy the shoes, to how they gained their cult status, and the long list of fashion-forward celebs with a pair of Manolos in their wardrobe, here is everything you need to know about the Manolo Blahnik Hangisi shoes.

When did Kate Middleton wear the Manolo Blahnik Hangisi shoes?

Kate wore the Manolo Blahnik Hangisi pumps with her go-to party gown

The Duchess of Cambridge wore the Manolo Blahnik Hangisi satin pumps in the portrait of herself and Prince William, which was unveiled at the University of Cambridge’s Fitzwilliam Museum. In the painting, Kate teamed the stunning satin pumps with her The Vampire’s Wife metallic midi dress in the most elegant forest green shade.
