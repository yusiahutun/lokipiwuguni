Tennis star hits back at trolls criticising her 'giant boobs' and 'unathletic body'

Tennis star Amanda Anisimova has hit back at her critics after one troll commented on her body, calling it "unathletic" and saying she has "giant boobs".

The 22-year-old was also told she has "narrow shoulders" by a person online as she posted the criticism on her Instagram story.

The Russian-American achieved great success early on in her tennis career, having won her first WTA title at the Copa Colsanitas in 2019 before the reaching the semi-final of the French Open later on that year at the age of 17.

However, Anisimova has since struggled to capitalise on her momentum after experiencing the tragic death of her father in 2019.

She did reignite her form last year, after winning another singles title as well as making the quarter-final of Wimbledon that resulted in her return to the top 25 players in the world.

At just 22, Anisimova is still so early in her career but has had to contend with criticism online with people targeting her body.

After she posted a mirror selfie to her Instagram story on a few weeks ago she was instantly met with a private message that disparaged her body.

Anisimova candidly showed a message which read: “The most unathletic body ever! Narrow shoulders and giant boobs”

The current world number 46 responded with a: a “thank you” accompanied with a kissing emoji, as she wrote "Idk what to think? Lol", above the post.

“The amount of people who comment on my body every day on social media… will never understand what the hyper fixation is about," she said.

In another story she posted on Instagram, Anisimova shared a photo of herself blowing a kiss at the camera as she wrote: “But enjoy your life, I’ll go enjoy mine.”
