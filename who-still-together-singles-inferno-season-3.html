Whos Still Together From Single's Inferno Season 3? Couples Now 2024

Spoilers ahead for Single’s Inferno season 3 finale. So many twists and turns during this season that were truly unexpected. After the shocking finale, many fans of this Korean dating show might want to know who in Single’s Inferno season 3 are still together.

Single’s Inferno debuted in 2021 and skyrocketed to be one of Netflix’s most-watched reality TV shows due to the shocking and dramatic nature. 12 contestants are put on a deserted island called “Inferno” and compete through challenges to couple up. Once a desired couple is matched, they are sent to “Paradise” which is a fancy luxury hotel where they could be able to get to know one another. Along with all the suspense and pairing, a set of panelists comment on the show including comedian Hong Jin-kyung, actress Lee Da-hee, Super Junior member Kyuhyun, rapper Hanhae, and YouTuber and season 2 alum Dex.

In an interview with Netflix Tudum, producer Kim Na-hyun shared if there’s room for the cast to be in touch with each other after they leave the island. “I see them interacting on Instagram, so I think it’s safe to say that they’re still in contact. Because they spent eight nights and nine days on an island together, where they not only built relationships but also friendships, they are probably still close.”

Season 3 started with two separate Infernos happening at the same exact time—leading to different merges, interesting choices, and most importantly a whole lot of drama.

In the last episode of season 3, four couples left Inferno together. While four people remained in Inferno including Son Won-ik, Cho Min-ji, Yun Ha-bin, and Yun Ha-jeong. So which of the Single’s Inferno couples are still together? We investigated below.

  • Who's still together from Single's Inferno Season 3?

    Read on for which Singles Inferno Season 3 couples are still together.

  • Are Gwan-hee and Hye-seon still together after Single's Inferno season 3?

    Are Gwan-hee and Hye-seon still together after Single’s Inferno season 3? It’s unknown whether or not Gwan-hee and Hye-seon are still together after the show ended. Gwan-hee posted a photo of the cast members and some fans on Reddit noticed that Hye-seon posted a comment that translated to, “the pic is too dark, I told you if you want to post lmk first so I can add filter” and deleted it quickly.

    Gwan-hee has been a source of drama in the show with the kinds of games he plays with his love interests, but he decided to settle down with Hye-seon after an emotional conversation during one of the last days on the island. I felt like I would keep thinking about Hye-seon if I picked Min-ji but I didn’t think I would think about Min-ji as much if I picked Hye-Seon. So in the end, I didn’t hesitate in picking Hye-Seon.”

  • Are Si-eun and Min-woo still together after Single's Inferno season 3?

    Are Si-eun and Min-woo still together after Single’s Inferno season 3? It’s unknown whether of not Si-eun and Min-woo are still officially together. However, fans noticed that Min-woo has been posting fan edits of the endgame couple on Instagram—leaving them to speculate that they’re still going strong.

    Min-woo and Si-eun made the panelists gush after their exchange at the end of their stay at Inferno, “You look like a princess.” Si-eun replied, “Then you’re my prince.”

  • Are Gyu-ri & Min-kyu still together after Single's Inferno season 3?

    Are Gyu-ri & Min-kyu still together after Single’s Inferno season 3? It’s not known whether or not Gyu-ri and Min-kyu are still together as they haven’t posted pictures of each other on their Instagrams. They also do not follow each other’sInstagram accounts.

    Gyu-ri picked Min-kyu after believing he was a steady man.  “I’ll get you plenty of blueberry pies once we’re out,” Min-kyu told her as they were leaving together. Gyu-ri asked, “How many?” Min-kyu said, “As many as you want. Until you can’t eat anymore.” 

  • Are Jin-seok & Min-young still together after Single's Inferno season 3?

    Are Jin-seok and Min-young still together after Single’s Inferno season 3? It’s unknown whether or not Jin-seok and Min-young are still together after leaving Inferno together during the finale.

    After the finale aired on Netflix, Jin-seok proudly posted a picture of himself at the cafe where he worked on his Instagram story. He also posted an Instagram post at a Single’s Inferno watch party with the cast with a photobooth picture of him standing firm with Ha-jeong. Meanwhile, Min-young has not posted anything on her Instagram regarding their relationship.

    During the final moments of the last episode, Min-young said, “This is nerve-wracking. Oppa, thank you for taking such good care of me here. This wouldn’t have been an actual inferno if I didn’t have you for the ride” adding that she wishes him to bake her bread in his bakery.
