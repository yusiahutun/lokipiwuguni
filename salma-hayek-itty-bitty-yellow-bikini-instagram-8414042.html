Salma Hayek Ignores Winter While Wearing an Itty-Bitty Yellow Bikini

In the summer, it's likely you'll catch Salma Hayek wearing a bikini more often than you will actual clothes (hello, red-hot suits and animal print one-pieces), and apparently, not even a change in season will stop her from sporting her favorite swimwear.

On Sunday, the actress shared a new bikini-clad photo from her vacation somewhere that's warmer than here. In the post captioned, "#sundaymood,” Salma modeled a mood-lifting multicolored yellow floral patterned two-piece featuring an itty-bitty bustier top with a front tie and coordinating cheeky bottoms. The actress posed against a rock wall while she accessorized her vibrant swimsuit with massive black cat eye sunglasses and a fresh tan.

She pulled her wet brunette hair back into a messy top knot hairstyle and had on little-to-no makeup.

Salma’s much-needed R&R post comes just days after a busy month of red-carpet appearances. Last week, the actress attended the 3rd Annual Gala for the Academy Museum of Motion Pictures in a custom white Gucci gown paired with a metallic clutch, silver jewelry by Boucheron, and a diamond-encrusted dainty headband. And before that, she and her husband, François-Henri Pinault, attended Balenciaga’s pre-fall 24 show in Los Angeles. Looking like a Balenciaga Barbie, Salma accessorized her slinky LBD with bright pink pumps and a pink quilted handbag.

Meanwhile, last month, she kicked off the holiday season in a shimmery emerald green custom Bottega Veneta gown featuring a twisted halter neckline with a bow at the Baby2Baby Gala. A pair of diamond drop Boucheron earrings and several diamond rings added even more sparkle to her party-ready dress.
