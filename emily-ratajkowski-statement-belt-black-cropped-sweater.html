Emily Ratajkowski Looks Like a Spy in Cropped Sweater and Statement Belt

Emily Ratajkowski looked like the most fashionable spy on the planet in a new series of posts on Instagram.

The model just shared a clip in whch she posed in a form-fitting turtleneck crop top from Mirror Palais, which cut off right at her chest and featured oversize long sleeves. To match the top, Ratajkowski wore a pair of straight-leg dress pants, also in black, cinched with a large leather statement belt from Khaite that featured shiny golden hardware. Ratajkowski added a few other pops of gold with her giant hoop earrings and a series of sparkling diamond rings on her fingers.

Turns out, these photos were taken as part of a press day for Viktor & Rolf. (Ratajkowski is one of the faces of the brand’s Flowerbomb fragrance). Besides her Kim Possible–esque ensemble, EmRata also had quite the stunning beauty look, with rose-gold eye shadow and dusty pink lipstick, courtesy of makeup artist Hung Vanngo. Meanwhile, the My Body author rocked a heavy side-part and big blown-out waves courtesy of hairstylist Jennifer Yepez.

courtesy of Emily Ratajkowski via Instagram//Instagram

“Back to work,” Ratajkowski wrote in her caption. And we’re certainly glad she’s back to bringing us more stylish fits.

Before 2023 was over, the Gone Girl actor shared a few other memorable ensembles on Instagram, including a Miu Miu leather set that featured a cropped button-up jacket and low-rise booty shorts that exposed her underwear. And around Christmastime, EmRata modeled a festive ribbed knit wrap sweater in maroon, which she paired with camo cargo pants.
