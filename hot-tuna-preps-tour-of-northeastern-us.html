Hot Tuna Preps Tour Of Northeastern US

NEW YORK (CelebrityAccess) — American blues-rock outfit Hot Tuna will be touring the Northeast in July, first as an acoustic duo and later with drummer, Justin Guip, as the Electric Hot Tuna Trio.

The tour starts at Jonathan's in Ogunquit, ME on July 9th and wraps up at the Great South Bay Music Festival, Patchogue, NY on July 19th.

Hot Tuna, a spin-off project of Jefferson Airplane, features Jorma Kaukonen on guitar and Jack Casady on the bass. The band's latest album "Steady As She Goes" was released in 2011 on Red House Records. – Staff Writers

The full list of tour dates
July 09 Jonathan's, Ogunquit, ME
July 10 The Flying Monkey, Plymouth, NH
July 11 Cabot Theater, Beverly, MA
July 12 Payomet PAC, North Truro, MA
July 14 Infinity Music Hall & Bistro, Norfolk, CT
July 16 Prudential Hall, New Jersey PAC, Newark, NJ
July 17 State Theatre, State College, PA
July 18 The Capitol Theatre, Port Chester, NY
July 19 Great South Bay Music Festival, Patchogue, NY
