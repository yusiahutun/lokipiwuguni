IOMA Paris Launches at Boots

Photography: Courtesy of IOMA Paris 

This September, bespoke skincare brand IOMA is launching nationwide at Boots across the UK.

Renowned for its advanced skin science and unique formulas, IOMA Paris’ full collection of skin and body care, along with their IOMA Sphere and IOMA Link diagnostic devices, will be stocking the shelves at Boots nationwide.

First launching in Paris in 2010, IOMA become one of the best-selling premium skincare lines in France, and is now available across the globe in 15 countries. The brand was responsible for the first skincare product with diagnostic technology embedded within it, earning it a spot in Bazaar’s Hot 100 round-up of the best beauty products last year.

What we love about IOMA is that the products are uniquely designed to cater to your specific skin type, making it the closest personalised skincare treatment available without seeing a specialist.
