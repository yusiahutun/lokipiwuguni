See All the Amazing Looks From the 2023 SAG Awards Red Carpet

With every passing weekend, we get closer and closer to the close of this year's awards season. And while it may seem like we've been seeing more red carpets than ever before, there's still more to come, starting with tonight's Screen Actors Guild Awards. For the 29th annual celebration, this year's nominees are celebrating each other — the SAG Award are voted on by each nominee's peers, after all. Of course, that means everyone showing up and showing out, from the stars of HBO's The White Lotus (star Haley Lu Richardson is actually this one of this year's ambassadors) to internet darling Sheryl Lee Ralph from ABC's Abbot Elementary.

While the SAG Awards may not be top of mind as people wrangle their Oscar ballots and still figure out what happened between Lizzo at the Grammy Awards and Rihanna's Super Bowl performance, the show has offered up major moments from stars like Lady Gaga and that time Jennifer Lopez and Ben Affleck skipped the show as rumors swirled about their relationship this time last year.

Keep scrolling for all the best looks from this year's Screen Actors Guild Awards red carpet.

Jamie Lee Curtis

Nominated for Outstanding Performance by a Female Actor in a Supporting Role for her role in Everything Everywhere All at Once, Jamie Lee Curtis arrived in a long-sleeved red gown with a plunging neckline from Romona Keveza.

Kathryn Newton

Bona fide fashion star Kathryn Newton channeled Old Hollywood icon Audrey Hepburn with this full-skirted look from Carolina Herrera — which included a necklace, something many stars (and stylists) have been eschewing at big events. Jewels from Cartier's Étincelle, Panthere, and Pluie collections added sparkle to her outfit.

Haley Lu Richardson

Ambassador and presenter Haley Lu Richardson wore a pearl-encrusted strapless Carolina Herrera gown and carried a coordinating handbag for this year's SAG Awards. Maison Boucheron jewelry finished her look.

F. Murray Abraham

HBO's The White Lotus is up for Outstanding Performance by an Ensemble in a Drama Series (and is a favorite to win). Richardson's co-star F. Murray Abraham arrived in a color that's not often seen on the red carpet: brown.

Adam DiMarco

Fellow White Lotus star Adam DiMarco wore a classic double-breasted look.

Sheryl Lee Ralph

Abbot Elementary star Sheryl Lee Ralph wore a shimmering Tadashi Shoji gown with floor-grazing split sleeves. Fernando Jorge earrings finished her look.

Jenny Slate

Jenny Slate wore an off-the-shoulder gown from Zuhair Murad’s Resort 2023 collection with an intricate bra-style bodice and a keyhole cutout. She finished her look with jewelry from Graziela.

Lisa Ann Walter

Ralph's co-star, Lisa Ann Walter, wore a gown with a full skirt and voluminous sheer sleeves.

Hong Chau

Chau, who is nominated for her role in The Whale, wore a Fendi couture dress with a fully embellished skirt, lace trim, and delicate train. She added diamond-and-pearl earrings from Mikimoto.

Meghann Fahy

Fahy wore a long white column with a dramatic side cutout and an asymmetric neckline by Ralph Lauren. Cartier's Reflection de Cartier earrings and Love rings finished off her look.

Theo James

And Fahy's White Lotus husband wore a classic black suit.

Britt Lower

Lower and her Severance co-stars are nominated for Outstanding Performance by an Ensemble in a Drama Series.

Meg Stalter

Hacks star Meg Stalter wore high boots and a corset with a gathered, printed skirt.

Danielle Deadwyler

Deadwyler (and her scalloped Louis Vuitton gown) is up for Outstanding Performance by a Female Actor in a Leading Role for her role in Till.

Cara Delevingne

Model-turned-actress Cara Delevingne wore a dramatic long-sleeved Carolina Herrera gown with a full skirt. Her show, Only Murders in the Building, is nominated for Outstanding Performance by an Ensemble in a Comedy Series. She added sparkle with jewelry from De Beers.

Harry Shum Jr

Everything Everywhere All at Once star Harry Shum Jr. traded his raccoon puppet for an all-black tuxedo.

Stephanie Hsu

Hsu, who is nominated for Outstanding Performance by a Female Actor in a Supporting Role for Everything Everywhere All at Once wore a long dress with a gathered bustle, which let her flash her leg without the need for a high slit. A ring, earrings, and bracelet from Cartier High Jewelry earrings added sparkle to her look.

Ayo Edebiri

Edebiri and her Pal from The Bear are nominated for Outstanding Performance by an Ensemble in a Comedy Series. She wore a standout checkerboard gown from Emilia Wickstead for tonight's ceremony. Simon G. jewelry finished her look.

Adina Verson

Severance star Dichen Lachman wore a classic three-piece suit.

Janelle James

Abbott Elementary's Janelle James wore a sheer Rodarte gown with lace detailing.

Ben Whitehair

Ben Whitehair, SAG-AFTRA's executive vice president, wore a bold, royal blue tuxedo.

Li Jun Li

Babylon's Li Jun Li wore a shimmering, sheer, scoop-neck gown from Fendi's couture collection. De Beers jewelry added even more shimmer to her embellished gown.

Ariana DeBose

Ariana DeBose, fresh off being the meme of the week post-BAFTAs, wore a longline pink double-breasted jacket and flared pants. Her look included jewelry from Melinda Maria and Effy.

Niecy Nash

Niecy Nash wore a neon-green Vera Wang gown with a sheer overlay.

Jessica Chastain

Chastain wore a wrap-style Zuhair Murad gown in a bold pink. The floor-sweeping gown featured a full gown and an off-the-shoulder silhouette.

Quinta Brunson

Brunson's dress served mermaid in a whole new way. Instead of a trumpet silhouette, her Jean-Louis Sabaji dress had a dramatic seashell design on the bodice.

Zendaya

Proving again that nobody does it like Zendaya, the star arrived at the SAG Awards in a rose-embellished Valentino gown and Bulgari jewels.

Simona Tabasco

The White Lotus breakout star wore a bright teal dress with a thick belt.

Amanda Seyfried

Seyfried's sleek take on the high-low trend was accentuated with her sky-hair hair. Her green dress is by Prada. She accessorized with Cartier Sixième Sens High Jewelry earrings and a Cartier High Jewelry bracelet.

Jenna Ortega

Ortega's super-shimmering, anything-but-basic black Atelier Versace gown had an asymmetric bodice and high-low hem.

Michelle Williams

Williams wore a Dior haute couture gown with a sculptural neckline and bow detail.

Viola Davis

Star of The Woman King Viola Davis wore an eye-catching yellow Valentino gown with long sleeves.

Andrew Garfield

Andrew Garfield wore David Yurman jewelry, no tie, and a sleek double-breasted Valentino suit.

Eddie Redmayne

After a one-piece tuxedo jumpsuit at the BAFTAs, Redmayne wore wide velvet pants and a white shirt with an oversized bow detail. The full look was from Saint Laurent.

Jessica Williams

Two Dope Queens and Shrinking star Jessica Williams wore a Rosie Assoulin gown with sheer panels and geometric swirls. Jewelry from Fred Leighton and Kwiat topped off her look.

Rooney Mara

Ronney Mara wore a gown with an intricate avian design made of tiny pieces of sheer lace by Alexander McQueen.

Elizabeth Debicki

The Crown's Elizabeth Debicki wore a long velvet Dior gown.

Claire Foy

Claire Foy, who stars in The Crown, wore a long Prada gown with a deep-V neckline.

Michelle Yeoh

Yeoh's Schiaparelli gown featured crinkle detailing all down the front. She finished her look with Moussaieff high jewelry.

Angela Bassett

Angela Bassett did the thing in a bright yellow Giambattista Valli gown with a huge trumpet skirt. De Beers jewelry added major sparkle to her already over-the-top look.

Aubrey Plaza

Plaza wore a super-sexy dress with a high slit and crisscross bodice.

Diego Calva

Babylon star Diego Calva wore a chocolatey-brown velvet jacket and a beige bow tie.

Ke Huy Quan

Ke Huy Quan wore a classic tuxedo to this year's show. His showstopping brooch was from De Beers.

Ana de Armas

The star of Blonde skipped out on any and every Marilyn Monroe reference with a sleek Louis Vuitton gown that featured shoulder bows and a floral print.

Cate Blanchett

Cate Blanchett's gown combined shimmering sequins and lace.

Julia Garner

Garner's long-sleeved dress featured a lingerie-inspired cup bodice and ruffled sleeves.

Austin Butler

Red carpet, red suit. Austin Butler's three-piece matched the carpet but still channeled Elvis's rock-star energy with flared pants.

Jeremy Allen White

The star of The Bear wore a classic double-breasted suit.

Jennifer Coolidge

Jennifer Coolidge arrived in a long jersey gown from Saint Laurent.
