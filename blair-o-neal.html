Biography

Blair O’Neal was born in Macomb, IL on May 14, 1981. Professional golfer, as well as a model in runway fashion shows, television hosting, print ads, catalogs, magazine covers, websites, and commercial advertisements. She competed on the Golf Channel’s Big Break: Prince Edward Island, where she was runner-up, and Big Break: the Dominican Republic, where she was the winner. She was a two-time contestant on The Big Break, co-hosted by Tom Abbott. She earned a sponsorship with Cobra Puma along with other golfers such as Rickie Fowler. Her father enrolled her and her brother in a local junior golf clinic when she was 11 years old. By the age of 13, she began playing for the American Junior Golf Association, and by the time she was in high school, had already established herself as one of the best junior golfers in the state of Arizona. She won the Women’s 5A Arizona State Golf Championship.
