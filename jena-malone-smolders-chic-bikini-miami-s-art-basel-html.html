Jena Malone smolders in a chic bikini at Miami's Art Basel

After a busy promotional tour for The Hunger Games: Mockingjay Part 1, Jena Malone decided to take some time to enjoy the Miami sun.

Following a performance with her band The Shoe at The Thompson Hotel Miami, the 30-year-old was seen relaxing on the Florida beach on Thursday.

While band mate Lem Jay Ignacio remained clothed, Malone slinked on the sands in a gingham bikini and a sheer black maxi dress.

Downtime: Jena Malone enjoyed Miami's beach during Art Basel on Thursday

Her performance during Art Basel was an extension of the duo's free summer tour at the Thompson Hotels of the world in between working on blockbuster films. 

The multi-talented star recently revealed to Us Weekly that knowing she will never portray the character Johanna Mason again was the most difficult part of wrapping the four-part trilogy.  

Sands style: Even when she was taking a dip, the star showed off her effortless fashion sense in a sheer maxi dress and gingham bikini

The Shoe: When Jena isn't acting, she is performing in her band, The Shoe, pictured here with band mate Lem Jay Ignacio

'Knowing that it was the end, that funny pressure builds inside the body when you’re shooting your final scene,' she said.

'It’s like, "Oh my goodness, I’m saying goodbye to a character that I’ve fallen deeply and madly in with for the past two years."'

After the Hunger Games: Malone's next film role will be as Hope Harlingen in Inherent Vice

Along with continuing her create music, Malone's next acting project is, Inherent Vice, is due out in theaters on January 9.

Jena stars alongside Hollywood heavyweights like Joaquin Phoenix, Reese Witherspoon, and Owen Wilson to bring the beloved novel to the big screen.

Set in the 1970s, the adaptation will tell the story of a In Los Angeles detective Larry "Doc" Sportello (Phoenix) who must find a couple of missing characters, including the husband (Wilson) of Hope Harlingen (Malone).
