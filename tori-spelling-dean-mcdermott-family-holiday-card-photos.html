Tori Spelling Reveals Family Holiday Card -- And Dean McDermott Is In It!

Tori Spelling is focused on keeping her family together!

The "True Tori" star released her annual holiday card on Wednesday, and the entire brood is featured in the photo, including husband Dean McDermott, and the couple's four kids Liam, Stella, Hattie, and Finn.

"I have so many amazing and supportive people in my life that I'm super grateful for, and during the holidays I like to remind them how much I truly love and appreciate each and every one of them," she wrote on her EdiTORIal blog. "And with 4 kids, finding time to put together our family holiday card can be daunting...but not this year!!"

The actress has had a tough year, following McDermott's affair with Emily Goodhand. The aftermath of the cheating scandal and the pair's rocky relationship was documented on their reality show, "True Tori."

While many wouldn't want to air their dirty laundry to the public, Tori says filming the show has been cathartic.

"I personally like doing it, for me it works," she told Today's Hoda Kotb and Kathie Lee. "I know it's individual, it's not for everyone. I came from a family that didn't talk about emotions, we repressed things. This was a vehicle for me to feel like a safe atmosphere where I could share and it's cathartic. It's therapy for me."

In fact, Dean recently admitted that he wants to quit the couple's reality show.

"I'm not going to continue with the show," Dean told Access Hollywood Live last week. "We have five more shows left... I can't do this anymore. I can't keep opening a vein, opening my soul and sharing my feelings and thoughts and demons with the world."

He isn't happy with the way Tori -- and the show -- portrays him as a bad guy (go figure!). Check out the clip above to see him confront his wife about his negative public image.
