Marisa Zanuck Biography, Age, Height, Husband, Net Worth

Marisa Zanuck is an American Reality star and a Real Estate Agent. She has established her own company called The Zanuck Group and also became a member of Sotheby’s International Realty. Along with that, she gained fame by appearing on The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills season 3.

Early Life

Marisa Zanuck was born on 6th March 1974 in Beverly Hills, California, United States. The name of her father is Richard Zanuck while her mother’s name has not been shared. Her grandfather’s name is Darryl Zanuck. She has also not shared details about his siblings with the media. Marisa has completed her high school education at a local school in the United States. She has not shared details about the same. For her further education, she enrolled herself at Arizona State University. Over there she received a Bachelor of Arts and B.A. Communication.

Marisa Zanuck Career

Marisa started her career as a Real Agent. In 1996 she worked with Hilton & Hyland as a Real Estate Broker in Beverly Hills for 17 years. After three years she received a real estate broker license. Later in 2013, she worked with Next Seekers/ Zanuck International as a Licensed Real Estate Broker and Managing Director. Apart from working in real estate, she also received an offer to appear in a reality show. In 2012 she was seen in TV series named The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills and next year in Million Dollar Listing Los Angeles. After 4 years she was working with Conscience VC as a Real Estate Broker.

Social Media

Marissa is an active user of a social media application like Twitter. In December 2016 she created her Twitter account and has earned 799 followers and 1,435 followings. She posts a lot of content about her real estate projects and deals with projects residing in the Greater Los Angeles Area. On the other hand, she also owns an Instagram account but is not so active on the same. She has 432 followers and her feed is filled with pictures of her children and lifestyle.
