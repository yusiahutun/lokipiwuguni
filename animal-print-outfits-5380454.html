12 Bold Animal Print Outfit Ideas

Animal prints can be anything you need them to: They can fit in nicely with a neutral capsule wardrobe, be a maximalist's dream, or act as that little bit of spice needed to enhance your closet. Whether it’s through a tiny accessory or a head-to-toe look, it's time to break out of your comfort zone and put together some animal print outfits. Let us inspire you through the 12 animal print outfit ideas ahead.

Keep It Simple

The easiest way to wear animal print is to find an easy dress and accessorize as you normally would. Strapless tube dresses couldn't be more of the moment and look great with chunky sandals and cool shades. 

Pair With Big Accessories


Animal print makes a statement, so why not level up your accessory game, too? It’s fun to fully commit to maximalism with chunky jewels as fun as the print itself. 

Contrast Against Brights 

Another way to stand out is to pair a print against a bright, bold color. It says "look at me" without feeling forced. Whether it’s a leopard tank under a bold suit or zebra clutch against a bright green dress, there are so many ways to do it.

Stick With the Basics

Sometimes the best outfits come from sticking with simple items in your closet. Pair a basic animal print turtleneck with a tank dress to create a cool layered look, or give your graphic tee a fashion-forward twist by styling it with a snakeskin skirt.

Grab a Suit

 A matching printed suit is actually one of the easiest ways to pull off animal print and somehow, it’s always in style. It’s a one-and-done move that feels elevated yet funky. Dress it up with strappy heels and a basic neutral (or nothing) underneath. Bonus points that it’s a versatile two-piece that can later be worn as separates. 

Play With Textures

Animal prints are fun and flirty, so make sure your completed ensemble matches that same energy. Instead of layering your prints with basic closet essentials, go for items with texture, like wearing animal print boots with a knit sweater and sequin skirt. 

Keep It Casual

Is there anything better than an animal print piece you can dress up or down? We'd argue that the answer is no. Invest in an oversized sweater, then pair it with boyfriend jeans and you're essentially ready to go.

Embrace Longer Silhouettes

We love our Y2K minis, but a longer print dress worn with heels can add both fun and elegance to your look. Plus, it's an easy day-to-night look that doesn’t feel too over the top.

Style From the Bottom Up

If you get stumped trying to incorporate the trend into your everyday outfit, start with statement bottoms. Once you've selected your animal print pants or skirt, you can easily build from there, adding solid clothing to balance things out, or other patterns to create something extra eye-catching.

Use Neutrals as a Base

If your goal is to keep things on the tamer side, make sure the print you choose aligns with your color palette. Play with black and white, then pile on accessories—like a leopard belt—for added interest.

Tie in a Blazer

If you don't have the energy to put together an elaborate OOTD, a blazer will come in handy, providing that extra pop while pulling your outfit together. Plenty of blazers feature animal print designs, so finding a sharp one to add to your rotation won't be hard.

Versatility Matters

A good rule of thumb? Search for animal print pieces that you can continue to recycle and re-wear with most of your wardrobe. Find something that's more unique than simple, this way it will amp up whatever you pair it with — even your everyday denim jacket.
