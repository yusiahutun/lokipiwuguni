Vanessa Hudgens Wears a Baby Bump-Hugging Dress

Vanessa Hudgens continues to nail the elevated casual maternity aesthetic with her latest date night outfit.

Last night, the High School Musical star took to Instagram to share her outfit from a date night with husband, baseball player Cole Tucker. Opting for a form-fitting brown maxi dress, Hudgens made her baby bump the star of the show. With its off-the-shoulder design and its dangerously high thigh-slit, the dress had the perfect level daring and drama for a romantic evening out.

Hudgens accessorized with a chunky gold bracelet, which she wore over her sleeve, and added a pop of color with her ultra-fierce highlighter yellow talon manicure. She tossed her hair up in a tousled, messy updo to complete the look.

This isn't the first time Hudgens has made the case for bodycon maternity wear. While some celebs opt to hide their bumps beneath voluminous, flowy dresses, Hudgens loves a form-fitted number that shows off her bump. Just take her ultra-chic black turtleneck dress by Vera Wang, which she wore on the Oscars red carpet to announce her pregnancy earlier this year.

Hudgens has also made the case for showing a little skin — pregnant or not. Her latest look, with its daring side slit, is just one example. At the Oscars after party, she opted for a heavenly Alberta Ferretti sheer black dress that revealed her black underwear and baby bump below.

It's great to see Hudgens embracing her body throughout her pregnancy — and making bold sartorial choices to show off each and every change she's going through. A true maternity wear icon!
