Bailey Chase is a famous stage and television actor from the United States. Bailey Chase is widely known for his portrayal as Butch Ada in the TV series Saving Grace, as Graham Miller in Buffy the Vampire Slayer, and as Cory Snyder in Walk. Ride. Rodeo.

Early Life and Childhood

Bailey Chase was born in Chicago, Illinois, United States on May 1, 1972. His full name is Bailey Chase Luetgert. He is 51 years old with the zodiac sign Taurus. Chase holds an American nationality and follows the Christian religion.

Regarding his family background, he is the son of Mike Luetgert (Father) and Melissa Bornquist (Mother). He grew up along with his brother Christopher George Luetgert.

Bailey Chase – Education

Speaking of his educational background, Chase graduated from a Jacksonville high school. Following that, he was given a football scholarship to play linebacker at Duke University.

Likewise, he earned a psychology degree upon graduation in 1995. At the London Academy of Music and Dramatic Art, he later received training.
