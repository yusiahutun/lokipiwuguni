Quick facts of King Bach

 Famous Canadian actor, Internet personality, and comedian King Bach.
  • King Bach was born on 26 June 1998(age-30) in Toronto, Ontario, Canada as Andrew B. Bachelor.
  • He holds Canadian nationality and belongs to the Cancer birth sign.
  • King was raised along with his younger sister named Christina Bach by his parents Byron Bachelor and Ingrid Mourice.
  • He studied at the Coral Springs Charter School. Later, he joined Florida State University and graduated from there in 2010 with a degree in business management.
  • After that, he went on to study at the New York Film Academy but dropped out from there in the last semester. Next, he studied improvisational theatre at The Groundlings.
  • Bach is an active Instagram, Facebook, Twitter, and YouTube personality. Until now, he garnered over 18.2 million followers on Instagram, 9.33 million+ followers on Facebook, 2.61 million+ followers on Twitter, and  2.2 million subscribers on YouTube.
  • He stands at a height of 5 feet and 8 inches with a pair of black eyes and black hair.
