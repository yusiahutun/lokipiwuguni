Zoey Deutch - Bio, Age, Net Worth, Career, Nationality, Facts

Table of Biography

Zoey Deutch is an American actress. Zoey Deutch gained fame for her roles as Maya Bennett on “The Suite Life on Deck” and Juliet Martin on “Ringer.” She is also Madelyn Deutch’s younger sister and the offspring of American filmmaker Howard Deutch and actress-director Lea Thompson.

Early Life and Education

Deutch was born in Los Angeles, California, on November 10, 1994. Zoey is 27 years old at the moment. She is Madelyn Deutch’s only sibling and older sister.
She had a “bat mitzvah” and is from a Jewish family.

Howard Deutch, her father, and Lea Thompson, her mother, make up her family. She received her diploma from Oakwood School. Later, she majored in theater while studying the arts at Los Angeles County High School.

Zoey Deutch – Career and Professional Life

In 2010, when she had a recurring part in Suite Life On Deck, Zoey Deutch started acting. She appeared in The Amazing Spider-Man in a small role as a talkative young woman in the restroom who is mistaken for a bug by the reptile. The DVD has her segment but the theaters didn’t screen it.

She appeared in the “The Girl In The Blue Mask” episode of Criminal Minds: Suspect Behavior. Additionally, Zoey appeared in Marc Cherry’s movie Thank Heaven. She has also performed performing roles alongside her mother Lea Thompson and sister Madelyn Deutch in the film Leader Cupcake.

Director Howard Deutch, the father of actress Zoey Deutch, is well-known for his roles in the films Some Kind of Wonderful and Pretty in Pink. Zoey appeared in the drama about dreams, sentiment, and dramatization called Wonderful Creatures in 2013 as a supporting character.

She was cast to play Tyson Ritter’s opposite in the 2014 January premiere of Gregg Allman’s biopic Midnight Rider, which is based on a true story. She co-starred in Richard Linklater’s movie Everyone Wants Some, which is about a group of understudies who live carefreely but still have to fulfill adult responsibilities. Paramount released the movie in April of 2016. She appeared in the official music video for Ed Sheeran’s song “Perfect,” from his critically acclaimed album Divide, released in 2017.

She also made an appearance in James Franco’s The Disaster Artist that same year. It was revealed in July 2018 that she would play the lead role in the brand-new American comedic online series The Politician. The program debuted on Netflix. Additionally, she appeared in Zombieland 2, the follow-up to Zombieland. It was published this year in October.
