The Fossil Pokémon

Tyrantrum is a Rock/Dragon-type Pokémon and is known as the “Fossil Pokémon.” It is believed to have roamed the earth during the prehistoric era, making it a unique and ancient species.

Formidable Size

One of the defining features of Tyrantrum is its imposing size. Standing at an impressive height of 8’2″ and weighing over 270 pounds, it is an intimidating sight to behold.

Massive Jaw Strength

Tyrantrum possesses an incredibly powerful jaw that can crush even the toughest of objects. Its bite force is said to be strong enough to pulverize boulders, making it a formidable opponent in battles.

Ancient Tyrant King

The name “Tyrantrum” is derived from the words “tyrant” and “tantrum,” alluding to its status as the king of tyrant dinosaurs. It ruled with dominance and commanded respect on the ancient earth.

Distinctive Crest

One of the most recognizable features of Tyrantrum is its large and elaborate crest on its head. This crest is believed to have played a role in intimidating adversaries and attracting mates.

Fierce Battle Abilities

Tyrantrum possesses a wide array of powerful battle abilities. Its ability “Strong Jaw” significantly boosts the power of biting moves, and its signature move “Head Smash” deals massive damage to opponents.

Ancient DNA Revival

Due to advancements in scientific technology, Tyrantrum is brought back to life through the revival of ancient DNA found in fossils. This process allows trainers to capture and train this ancient beast.

Ruler of the Pokedex

With its distinctive appearance and formidable strength, Tyrantrum has become a favorite amongst trainers who seek to complete their Pokedex. Its entry in the Pokedex often highlights its fearsome nature and dominance.

Dinosaur-Like Features

Tyrantrum’s design is heavily inspired by dinosaurs, particularly the Tyrannosaurus rex. Its sharp fangs, powerful legs, and overall physique resemble that of the mighty ancient predators.

Adaptable in Battle

Despite its ancient origins, Tyrantrum has proven to be versatile in battle, being able to learn various moves of different types. This adaptability allows trainers to customize its move set to suit their strategies.

Rare and Illusive

Tyrantrum is known for its rarity, making it a prized find for trainers. It is predominantly found in ancient fossil form, requiring careful restoration and revival before it can be encountered and captured.

A Symbol of Power

In Pokémon lore, Tyrantrum is often seen as a symbol of strength and power. Its presence commands respect and instills fear in those who dare challenge it, making it a force to be reckoned with.

FAQs

1. How can I obtain Tyrantrum?

Tyrantrum is an evolved form of Tyrunt, which can be obtained by reviving the Jaw Fossil found in the Kalos region. Once you have a Tyrunt, you can level it up to level 39 to evolve it into Tyrantrum.

2. What is Tyrantrum’s typing?

Tyrantrum is a dual-type Pokémon, being both Rock and Dragon-type. This unique combination gives it a wide range of moves and makes it resistant to certain types of attacks.

3. What are Tyrantrum’s signature moves?

Tyrantrum has two powerful signature moves: Head Smash and Dragon Tail. Head Smash is a devastating Rock-type move that deals massive damage but also causes recoil damage to the user. Dragon Tail, on the other hand, is a Dragon-type move that can force the opposing Pokémon to switch out.

4. What are Tyrantrum’s strengths and weaknesses?

Tyrantrum is strong against Flying, Bug, Fire, and Ice-type Pokémon. However, it is weak against Fighting, Ground, Steel, Dragon, Fairy, and other Rock-type moves. It’s important to consider these matchups when using Tyrantrum in battles.

5. Can Tyrantrum mega evolve?

No, unfortunately, Tyrantrum does not have a Mega Evolution form. However, its base form is already incredibly powerful and a formidable opponent in battles.
