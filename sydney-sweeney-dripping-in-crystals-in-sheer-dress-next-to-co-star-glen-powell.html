Sydney Sweeney'dripping in crystals' in sheer dress next to co-star Glen Powell

Actress Sydney Sweeney dazzled alongside her co-star Glen Powell during the premiere of their upcoming rom-com film Anyone But You.

The Euphoria star and Glenn, who plays her love interest, walked the red carpet in New York yesterday (November 11), and Sidney looked a vision in her outfit choice. The Hollywood star couldn’t help but steal the show on the Red Carpet.

Sidney Sweeney and Glen Powell dress up for their premiere

Sydney wore a floor-length dress that was silver-colored and covered in crystals to the New York event. She dazzled in the flashing camera lights as it hugged the curves of her body.

The dress was accessorized with a black belt under her bust, while her blonde locks were up in a neat bun. Her makeup was a neutral-toned glam that complimented her gown.

Glenn Powell stepped out alongside Sydney Sweeney and was out to impress with his outfit too. The 35-year-old donned a navy blue suit with a white T-shirt and black dress shoes.

A co-star love affair?

Over the past few months, there have been rumors swirling that Sydney Sweeney and Glen Powell were having an affair on set, despite having partners. The TV actress is engaged to Jonathan Davino, while Glen was with Gigi Paris until earlier this year. Their split didn’t help the cheating rumors.

They have batted away romance claims numerous times while doing press for their film. But they’ve denied it over and over again.

Glen told Men’s Health: “When all that stuff happened, you know, publicly, it felt disorienting and unfair. But what I’m realizing is that’s just a part of this gig now.”

Sydney, however, seemed more accepting of the rumor pool. She embraced the rumors where it came to her movie co-star.

“It’s a rom-com. That’s what people want! Glen and I don’t really care. We have so much fun together, and we respect each other so much,” she told Variety.

The celebrities seemed quite cozy yesterday (November 11) when the pair were interviewed on the Red Carpet. Glen Powell claimed he and Sydney Sweeney “loved each other” when the interviewer said that the pair had “chemistry.”

Despite giggles between the pair, Glenn told Hoda Kotb that there was nothing romantic between them.

He explained: “No, but we do love each other. And, honestly, this is one of the most spectacular humans I’ve ever met. She’s really great.”
