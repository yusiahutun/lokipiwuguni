Emma Willis nails romantic power dressing in Gucci's 2,600 trouser suit

The TV host wore the gorgeous outfit to a magazine party in London

    There are trouser suits, and then there are Gucci trouser suits. Emma Willis just showed us all how elegant power dressing is really done in one of the label's most divine outfits. The TV presenter wore a stunning red and navy Gucci floral trouser suit to 's 20th birthday party in London on Tuesday evening, and we'll think you'll agree, she looks amazing. The mum-of-three chose the chic outfit for the star-studded event attended by the likes of Millie Mackintosh, Anna Friel and Fearne Cotton. The price tag is quite something: £1,750 for the blazer and £850 for the matching trousers on matchesfashion.com.

    We love the feminine cut of the Gucci suit featuring cute pink and olive-green rose motifs. The single-breasted blazer is a cotton and wool blend, while the wide-leg trousers are high-rise and slim-fit with turn-up cuffs at the ankle. Emma teamed her suit with some fun candyfloss pink heels and a matching pink lipstick. The star went for a strong smoky eye makeup look and wore her hair in her trademark quiff style.
