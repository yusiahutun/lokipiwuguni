Microlinks pros and cons: Are the hair extensions good for you?

Microlinks are a trendy hair extension that is making rounds on social media platforms. They have been championed as the best alternative to wigs and weaves because they are light in weight and versatile. But before you pick up the phone to book an appointment with your hair stylist, you should weigh microlinks pros and cons to know if they are good for you.

Who doesn't want to look like a diva with long, flowing hair? The look is simple to achieve if you have long, natural hair. But hair extensions will do the trick if you don't have long hair. Synthetic hair extensions such as weaves and microlinks will add length and thickness to your short, natural hair.

What are microlinks?

Microlinks is a hair extension that looks almost similar to a wig. But, the hair stylist uses tiny copper or aluminium metal beads to attach individual hair extensions to small sections of your natural hair. Unlike other hair extensions, such as crotchet braids, microlinks do not require any braiding.

The hairstylist will part the hair into small sections and apply the beads at the root of each section. Like all the other hair extensions, microlinks have their own advantages and disadvantages. Here are all the details you need to know about microlinks pros and cons for black hair.

What are the benefits of microlinks?

There are several benefits of adding microlinks to your hair. These include:

1. They work on any hair type

One of the benefits of microlinks is that they can be applied to any hair type because they are blended into natural hair. But you need to ensure you choose the hair extension that matches your hair texture.

2. They are natural-looking

Microlinks also easily blend with your natural hair and are a natural-looking alternative to crotchet extensions.

3. Easy way to add volume and length

Microlink hair extensions are the ideal choice if you want to add volume and extra length to your natural hair. The hairstyle is quite versatile, and there are so many styles that have been created by hair stylists from different countries.

4. They can last up to 3 months

How long do microlinks last on natural hair? With proper maintenance, they can last for two to three months. But you should not keep the extensions in your hair for too long because they can cause hair damage.

What are the disadvantages of Microlinks?

Some of the downsides of getting micro links include:

1. It's not a protective style

If you are shopping for a protective hairstyle, microlinks are not a good option. The hairstyle requires you to regularly wash and condition your hair for best results.

You need to visit the hairstylist every few weeks so that the hair can be washed and hydrated. The hair stylist will also fix any beads that have started slipping.

Do microlinks grow your hair? You should not install microlinks to grow your hair because they do not promote hair growth. Your hair will grow at the same pace it would in its natural state.

2. It's expensive

You should also be prepared to dig deeper into your pocket to achieve this look. To install the microlinks, you need to purchase hair extensions and microlinks and pay the hairstylist.

3. Doesn't work on extremely short hair

Another disadvantage is that you cannot install microlinks on short hair. If you have short hair, you should wait for it to grow to at least four inches long before you can try this hairstyle.

4. Not the best hairstyle for weak hair

The hairstyle is also not ideal for weak hair because it can break it. If you experience excessive hair shedding, the weight of hair extensions will cause more damage. It is imperative that you consult your hairdresser to advise you on whether your hair is strong enough to handle the microlinks hair extensions.

5. The installation process is time-consuming

The hairstyle is also time-consuming. The process of attaching the beads to the separate sections of your natural hair can take between four and six hours, depending on the speed of the hairdresser.

Microlinks pros and cons are the only tools you have at your disposal to know if the hair extensions are good for you. The benefits of microlinks outweigh the drawbacks. As long as you can afford the installation cost, microlinks are a great hairstyle, and you should not think twice about trying it.
