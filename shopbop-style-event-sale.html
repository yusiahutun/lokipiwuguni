Best Shopbop Style Event Finds What to Shop at Shopbop's Sale

While the average shopper might be holding their cards—credit cards, that is—close to their chests in preparation for Black Friday and Cyber Monday, the seasoned shopper knows the magic starts right now. After all, as the saying goes, the early bird gets the well-dressed worm (or something like that).

From now until September 22, Shopbop’s Style Event is alive and oh so well. On thousands of select products, you can save 15 percent off orders over $200 and 20 percent off orders over $500. And for high rollers who spend more than $800, you’ll receive up to 25 percent off your total value. All you need to get in on the fun is to use the promo code STYLE at checkout. Some items you can save majorly on include Brandon Blackwood handbags, Simon Miller tops, and Ganni clogs.

Unsure where to begin? Don’t fret. Shop from our curated list of BAZAAR-approved picks below, all with your ever-growing fall wardrobe in mind. Whether it's a sweater dress worthy of taking from the office to date night or a pair of loafers that will go with just about everything you own, your next great closet find awaits. And if you’re in the market for more fall fashion ideas, be sure to shop our guides on the best sweaters, fall boots, wide-leg jeans, and more this season.
