Who Is Sheldon Bream? Bio, Job, Height, Net Worth, Brother, Age

Sheldon Bream and his wife Shannon Bream are receiving congratulatory messages from fans, friends, and family as she is replacing mainstream journalist and veteran anchor Chris Wallace who hosted Fox News Sunday. This article is all about Shannon’s husband Sheldon Bream. Here we address details regarding his job, height, job & net worth, famous brother, and his brain tumor. So, keep on scrolling down this Sheldon Bream Bio to learn more.

Meet Sheldon Bream, Shannon Bream Husband

Sheldon and Shannon Bream met each other at the university. They reportedly met while attending Liberty College and started dating not long after. At the time, she was a winner of the Miss Virginia beauty pageant in 1990. According to Woman Around Town, they were both dating other people when they were introduced during their senior year.

They reportedly exchanged their wedding vows on 30 December 1995. On their 26th anniversary in December 2021, Shannon tweeted, “We said “I do” 26 years ago today, and had no idea all the highs and lows we’d face. I thank God every day he put us on this journey together! Happy Anniversary, My Love”.

During an appearance on Tucker Carlson’s show in November 2016, Shannon revealed that she had been married for 21 years. She also reflected on the fact that she misplaced her wedding ring. She added that Sheldon was understanding about it but months passed with no sign of a ring.

However, the ring reemerged on thanksgiving, mixed in with an article of clothing she hadn’t worn in a while.

Meanwhile, the couple had their first date in February 1993. On their 25th first date anniversary, Shannon posted a picture after sneaking to have a quick bite with her husband that day. Sheldon, who is a romantic at heart, showed up wearing the same shirt he wore on their first date.

On Valentine’s Day 2022, Shannon posted, “Happy Valentine’s Day to my one and only, forever and always. Everything in my life is better because of you.”

Even after 26 years of marriage, they still don’t have any children together. But they are proud dog parents of a dog named Jasper. Previously they had a dog named Miss Mocha.

Sheldon Bream Age

Sheldon Bream was born in October 1970. As of Aug 2022, Sheldon is 51 years old.

Who Is Sheldon Bream Brother?

Sheldon Bream is the son of Jouetta M. Bream and Sylvester Bream Jr.

His father, Sylvester was born on 4 May 1930 in Cumberland County, Pennsylvania to Sylvester Daniel Bream and Cecile E Frost Bream. He was retired from Masland Industries, Carlisle; a veteran of the Korean War; and a member of Uriah United Methodist Church, Gardners.

Whereas, his mother, Jouetta was born on 7 September 1932 to Arthur and Freda (Killian) Crusey. She was a 1951 graduate of Boiling Springs High School. Moreover, She was employed by the Carlisle Shoe Company, Mt. Holly Springs Elementary School, where she was the head cook, Allen Dairy and Leo’s Dairy, and Giant Foods. Her most recent place of employment was Karns Foods.

While Sheldon’s father died on 25 May 1995 at the age of 65, his mother passed away on 9 February 2022 at the age of 89.

Sheldon Bream has two brothers; Stanley A. Bream of Dell City, Virginia, and Sid Eugene Bream of Wexford, Allegheny County.

Sid was a former professional baseball player for the Pittsburgh Pirates. Sid was born on 3 August 1960. He played professionally from 1983 to 1994.

The list of the teams he played for includes Los Angeles Dodgers (1983–85), Pittsburgh Pirates (1985–90), Atlanta Braves (1991–93), and Houston Astros (1994). His last appearance was on 27 July 1994. His batting average was .264, had 90 home runs, and 455 runs batted in.

Moreover, Sheldon also has three sisters; Laurie K. Stover of Mount Holly Springs, Christine M. Mumma of Enola, and Theresa A. Bream of Lynchburg, Virginia.

Sheldon Bream Brain Tumor

Sheldon Bream battled and survived a brain tumor. He was diagnosed with the disease when he was 24 years old. In January 2018, Shannon Bream tweeted, “Sending you all good wishes and prayers @MarkHyman. My husband is a healthy, strong, brilliant brain tumor survivor, and you will be too”.

In September 2018, she tweeted again, “Editing the chapter on my husband’s brain tumor diagnosis, surgery, and recovery today and it’s easily one of the two toughest chapters to re-live. It certainly puts me back in those dark days, but there’s a message of hope.”.

Eventually, in January 2020, Shannon opened up about it on Fox Nation’s Ride To Work. She said, “It was a complete shocker”. “He was a college athlete, very healthy and strong,” she told Fox Nation’s Abby Hornacek. “And he’d had an ear problem — like a ringing in his ear — for a long time.”

“They treated him for everything, ear infections, all kinds of stuff,” she continued. “And they said, ‘Well, we need to rule one thing out. It’s a longshot.’ But the test came back and they said ‘You’ve got a brain tumor. It’s about the size of a golf ball and it’s got to come out.'”

Sheldon underwent a nine-hour surgery to remove the tumor. “The tumor was clean. Got that out. Luckily benign. It was wonderful news,” she remembered. “But he had a complication in that all of the swellings from the brain surgery crushed some of his facial nerves and he ended up with severe paralysis.” At the time, Shannon was in law school. She and Sheldon were still starting their lives together and engaged to be married.
