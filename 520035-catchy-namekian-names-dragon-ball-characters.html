What are Namekian names based on?

What are all the Namekians named after? Namek, the name of the homeworld of the Namekians, is from the Japanese word "namekuji", meaning slug. Most, if not all, of the Namekian names have a musical theme, and their race is characterized by scaly, green skins and antennae on their heads.

The Namekian planet was home to more than 100 Namekians in the Dragon Ball series. But, an atmospheric wave drastically changed the planet's climate, leading to the death of many Namekians. But a few notable characters survived, such as Lord Slug and Kami.

What is nail meaning in Namekian?

Nail is described as the "lone" warrior in the manga. He is the personal bodyguard of Grand Elder Guru and is believed to be the strongest character.
