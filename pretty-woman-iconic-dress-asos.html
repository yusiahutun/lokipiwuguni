Pretty Woman's iconic dress is now available to buy on ASOS

Rex Features

Should you wish to dress up like Julia Roberts in Pretty Woman, you now can via swimwear brand Hunza G and ASOS.

The London-based label, best known for its popular swimwear, has teamed up with ASOS to create a capsule collection, starring the 'Pretty Woman' dress - an 80s-inspired, cut-out mini dress that requires toned abs and Amazonian legs. It even features the metal hoop that Roberts made famous in the film.

ASOS

ASOS

Although the online store is unable to give precise figures, a spokesperson for ASOS said that the reception has been positive and sales strong so far. The dress, priced at £136, is currently available in monochrome, but new colourways in blue and red are landing today.

Seeing as the line between good and bad taste is becoming increasingly blurred in fashion, ASOS and Hunza G may well be onto a good thing.
