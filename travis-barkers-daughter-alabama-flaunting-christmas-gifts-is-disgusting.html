Travis Barker's daughter Alabama 'flaunting' Christmas gifts is 'disgusting'

Travis Barker’s daughter, Alabama, has come under fire from fans for sharing her lavish Christmas gifts on Instagram, which she received this year.

Alabama Barker has been showered with Christmas gifts and birthday presents as the year comes to a close. The influencer and model, who recently turned 18, faced backlash for displaying the expensive presents she received. Alabama, who is Blink 182 star Travis Barker’s daughter, took photos of the lavish gifts and put them on Instagram.

Alabama Barker faces backlash for showing off Christmas gifts on Instagram

The former Pretty Little Thing model first posted the snaps on her Instagram, under her profile Alabama Luella Barker, believed to be on her Stories.

Reality TV star Kylie Jenner gifted her a Cartier silver watch, which Alabama uploaded, saying “Thank You”.

Then, fans got to see her music star dad bought her and her brother, Landon Barker, a black G-Wagon. Yep, that vehicle can set the celebrity back about $140,000 each – depending on whether he received a bargain or bought second-hand. But we don’t think he did that.

Alabama Barker didn’t stop there, she also boasted receiving her first Birkin bag – a favorite among Hollywood stars.

She held the luxury purse in her hand, and said: “Your girl got her first Birkin.”

Reddit fans say it’s a ‘disgusting display of wealth’

But followers weren’t happy with her supposedly bragging about her gifts.

Someone screenshot Alabama Barker’s Christmas and birthday gifts from Instagram and uploaded it onto Reddit.

One Redditer wrote: “The watch retails for $4,150. That’s definitely cheap for Kris Jenner.”

A second said: “I always think about how I would be immensely grateful to receive even a third of the money spent on their gifts, whereas they’re leaving it in a wardrobe and using it maybe once.”

Then a third added: “There was also a Chanel wallet from Kris. Don’t forget that.”

A fourth said: “These people really need to find a charity or something to donate to. It’s pretty disgusting how they flaunt there wealth when so many people are having such a hard time right now with the economy. People are starving.”
