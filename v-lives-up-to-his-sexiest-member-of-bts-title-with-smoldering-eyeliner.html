V lives up to his 'sexiest member of BTS' title with smoldering 'eyeliner'

BTS V is proving why he is called ‘the sexiest member’ of the group yet again, as a new magazine cover has fans celebrate the beginning of the ‘solo Kim Taehyung’ era.

V, aka Kim Taehyung of South Korean pop juggernauts BTS, is also the most popular Bangtan member on Instagram, where he boasts over 60.9 million followers. Along with his Instagram popularity, the music star is the official ambassador of luxury brands Celine and Cartier.

BTS V dubbed as the ‘sexiest member of BTS’ with smoldering ‘eyeliner’

BTS V is living up to his ‘sexiest member of BTS’ title in the latest Pop Magazine cover of September.

The celebrity icon had a smokey eye look and fringe leather jacket. V will be gracing the cover of three magazines in September including W Korea, Pop Magazine, and Arena Korea.

The Christmas Tree crooner has chosen different looks for all three magazines. In W Korea, the Cartier ambassador will be showing a multi-faceted portrait of his colorful persona and also open up about his life in an intimate interview. For Arena and Pop Magazine, V will be channeling his ‘dazzling symbol of youth’ persona in Celine clothes.
