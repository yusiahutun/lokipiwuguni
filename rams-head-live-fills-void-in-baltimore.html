Rams Head Live! Fills Void In Baltimore

BALTIMORE, MD (CelebrityAccess MediaWire) — In January 2005, Rams Head Promotions will unveil a world class, 1,800 capacity venue in
Baltimore, Maryland’s Inner Harbor. Bringing nearly a
decade of experience and an excellent track record with
successful, high profile entertainment, Rams Head Live!,
will emerge on December 15, 2004 as the Mid-Atlantic’s
highest quality entertainment venue, says Rams Head Live!.

It took over two-years of collaboration with top notch
industry professionals in lighting, sound, stage with award winning architects to develop Rams Head Live!

Rams Head Promotions says the $5 million dollar multi-level venue, Rams Head Live!,
features 26,000 square feet of architectural mastery.
The 3 levels of viewing area are highlighted with a 40
foot VIP mezzanine over-looking the stage below. With
over 40 monitors and a backdrop video screen, music
enthusiasts are assured an unmatched experience.
Premium artist amenities await artists at Rams Head
Live! featuring luxuriously decorated green rooms,
a personal in-house chef trained at internationally
recognized Johnson and Wales University, two wet bars
and lounging furniture for relaxation before, during and
after the show.

Located within minutes of BWI Airport and Interstates
95, 83 & 295, Rams Head Live! is easily accessible by
road or by plane. Rams Head Live! is situated within the
perimeters of Baltimore’s Power Plant Live!, a leading
entertainment project in the U.S. attracting more than
10 million visitors in 2003.

Reed S. Cordish, Vice President of Development, says “Rams Head Live! fills a total void in the marketplace for this size and caliber live music venue and puts Baltimore on par with Philadelphia, Washington D.C., and other major markets.”
