Rosie Huntington-Whiteley and Jason Statham Buy Beverly Hills Mansion Together

Rosie Huntington-Whiteley and her Furious 7 star beau Jason Statham purchased a five-bedroom mansion in Beverly Hills for just under $13 million (which they split in half, according to TMZ). Sparking engagement rumors, the real estate purchase marks the first home for the British couple, who recently celebrated their five year anniversary.

The elegantly modern pad located in California's coveted 90210 zip code features exposed wooden accents, secluded garden areas and scenic views. The 7,000-plus-square-foot property comes with five bedrooms, eight bathrooms, an eight-car garage and a waterfall pool—perfectly suited for a Victoria's Secret Angel.
