Havana Nights

Terri and Mike Pettengill

The festive “Havana Nights” event celebrated Latin dance, art and music. The evening included a special presentation of Flamenco dancing and an art exhibit featuring the works of Jesus Rivera and Robert Wong. David S. Millstone Arts Foundation coordinated “Havana Nights” to benefit the Arts Council of Martin County.
