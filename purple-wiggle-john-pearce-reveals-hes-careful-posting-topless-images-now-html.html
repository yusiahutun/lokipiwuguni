Purple Wiggle John Pearce reveals he's 'careful' about posting topless images now

John Pearce boasts a mixed entertainment career going from a member of Justice Crew to a Ninja Warrior star before transitioning into a children's entertainer. 

Now the 31-year-old hunk, who was appointed as a Purple Wiggle in 2021, says he has to be 'careful' about the 'thirst traps' he posts online. 

In an interview with Stellar magazine, which will release on Sunday, the former singer spoke about his online persona versus his image as a children's entertainer.

'Taking your shirt off and being a kid's entertainer is not in the same room. I'm totally aware of all that,' he told the publication.

'You know, we have to be super careful. People are entitled to their opinions and say what they like.'
