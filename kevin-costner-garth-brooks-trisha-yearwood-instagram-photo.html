See 'Yellowstone' Star Kevin Costner's Iconic Photo with Garth Brooks and Trisha Yearwood

Kevin Costner has been keeping busy as he awaits the season 4 premiere of Yellowstone! The actor and Dutton family patriarch has been traveling the U.S. with his country-rock band, Kevin Costner and the Modern West. Fans of his acting career may not know that Kevin is a singer-songwriter (he plays guitar too!). His band has been around since 2007, and in 2020, they released an album inspired by the show called Tales from Yellowstone, with 16 tracks mostly written from John Dutton's perspective.

The band's latest performance attracted some major celebrities! The "Tales from Yellowstone Tour" stopped in Nashville, Indiana, and country music superstars Garth Brooks and Trisha Yearwood took in the show. Kevin posted an epic photo from their meet-and-greet on his Instagram page, and fans were so excited to see three stars in one photo.

Kevin captioned the photo, "What a treat to have these legends come see our show. Thanks for coming out, @garthbrooks & @trishayearwood! #TalesFromYellowstone."
