Jessica Simpson has been in the spotlight for over 2 decades and has had quite the evolution over time. The Texas girl, born July 10, 1980, has transformed so much over the years, going from a bubbly pop princess to a full-blown fashionista with one of the most successful clothing brands in the business.

She started off embracing 2000s trends, rocking low-rise jeans and baby tees while singing beloved tracks like “I Think That I’m In Love With You” and “With You”. And of course, she became nearly synonymous with Daisy Duke short shorts when she played the hillbilly hottie in the 2005 remake of ‘The Dukes Of Hazzard’. Proving her true fashion prowess, she launched the Jessica Simpson Collection in 2005. The company has made over $1 billion to date.
