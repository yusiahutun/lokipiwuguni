Jason Aldean shows keen fashion sense as he packs on PDA wife Brittany in red dress at ACA Awards

The biggest and brightest in country music were on hand for the 58th Academy of Country Music Awardsat the Ford Center at The Star in Frisco, Texas.

Multi-platinum artist Jason Aldean had a busy night in store for himself as one of the performers to hit the big stage, as well as being nominated for Entertainer Of The Year.

But before getting in the mix of all the award presentations, the Macon, Georgia native took to the red carpet for a round or two of photos with his wife of eight-years, Brittany Aldean.

The couple were the picture of bliss as they got handsy with each other, while photographers yelled out for the duo to look their way.

The country singer, 46, could't resist packing on the PDA with his left arm wrapped around his lady's waistline dressed in a gray fitted blazer that he matched with a black shirt, pants and boots, while Brittany wowed admirers in her leggy red-feathered dress.

Packing PDA: Jason Aldean, 46, and wife Brittany Aldean, 34, wowed the crowd gathered along the red carpet for the 58th Academy Of Country Music Awards in Frisco, Texas 

The couple couldn't stopped smiling as they struck various poses for photographers.

Of course, the If I Didn't Love You star opted to throw in a black cowboy hat to his fashion ensemble, while his wife turned heads with her red dress that came complete with a short cut on the left, allowing her to flaunt her flawless gams.

The short cut was lined with feathers from top to bottom, while her plunging neckline let her show case her fab figure, including her ample cleavage.

The Charlotte, North Carolina native also wore a pair of white open-toe heels, and had her long blonde locks pulled back off her face and into a ponytail with a center part.

While turning slightly for the cameras that were  lined up along the red carpet, Jason continued to hold on tight to his wife.

And then just as a photographers asked for their attention, the singer turned to his left and gave Brittany a kiss on the lips.

In a move only an attentive wife could have been able to pull off, Brittany took notice that Jason had a little lipstick on his face, and she quickly wiped it away.

And despite the quick-thinking move, the couple didn't miss a beat, and continued on with their moment in the spotlight along the red carpet.

Make that move: While posing for demanding photographers, the If I Didn't Love You star turned to his left and gave his wife of eight years a kiss on the lips

Doting wife: After sharing their kiss, Brittany noticed her husband had lipstick on his face and she quickly wiped it away

Eventually, the couple would make their way to their seats, but Jason couldn't get comfortable for too long, since he had to prepare for his performance of his new song Tough Crowd.

Jason had some stiff competition in the Entertainer Of The Year category with the likes of Kane Brown, Miranda Lambert, Luke Combs, Carrie Underwood, Morgan Wallen and Chris Stapleton.

The singer came into the evening as a 12-time Academy Of Country Music Award winner, which included Top New Male Vocalist in 2005 not long after his debut self-titled studio album dropped at the beginning of his career.

Stylish couple: The country singer showed off his keen fashion sense in a gray blazer with black pants, hirt, boots and cowboy hat, while his wife stunned in a red dress with feathers lined along a short cut on the left

The musician has had 27 singles reach number one on either the Hot Country Songs  or the Country Airplay charts during his illustrious career. 

He's also had one of his albums, My Kinda Party (2010) get certified triple platinum by the Recording Industry Association of America (RIAA), and the album Night Train (2012), get recognized as double platinum.

The crooner has also had several other studio albums be certified single platinum by the RIAA, including Jason Albean (2005), Relentless (2007), Wide Open (2009), and Old Boots, New Dirt (2014).

The couple are the proud parent of two children, son Memphis, five, and daughter Navy, four.

Jason also has two other daughters with his first wife Jessica Aldean, Keeley, 20, and Kendyl, 15.
