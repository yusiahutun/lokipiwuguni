About

Sofía Castro is a 27 year old Mexican Actress. Born Angélica Sofía Castro Rivera on 30th October, 1996 in Mexico City, Distrito Federal, Mexico, she is famous for being the daughter of former First Lady and actress Angelica Rivera and telenovela producer José Alberto Castro and the stepdaughter of former president Enrique Peña Nieto, and for her roles as Kenia in the Netflix/Univision/Televisa crime drama series El Dragón: El regreso de un guerrero, Dafne Castro Herrera in the Televisa telenovela series Por Siempre Mi Amor, Eglantina in the Televisa telenovela series Esperanza del Corazón, and Lilia in the Televisa telenovela series Teresa in a career that spans 2010–present.
