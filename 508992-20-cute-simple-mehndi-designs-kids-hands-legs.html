20 cute and simple mehndi designs for kids hands and legs

Mehndi is quite important in Indian festivities. Everyone, including kids, middle-aged, and older people, decorate their bodies with intricate and cute mehndi designs. However, when dealing with children/ kids, the application is usually a bit complicated because they are too playful or clingy. That's why you should keep the mehndi designs for kids simple or minimal.

Cute and simple mehndi designs for kids make their small hands look cute and cuddly. There are many easy henna designs for kids, meaning you'll find one that looks incredible on yours.

Here are some fabulous mehndi designs for kids that'll bring glam and glitz to their cutie pie hands.

A floral-flower-like design with dots and some droplets will look great on your baby's hands. But babies have the habit of fidgeting their hands, so it is best to choose a minimalist pattern.
