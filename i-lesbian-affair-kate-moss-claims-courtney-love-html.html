I had lesbian affair with Kate Moss, claims Courtney Love

I bedded Kate Moss, claims Courtney Love

By Ben Todd
Created: 06:08 EDT, 20 May 2010

72

She has dated rock stars, Hollywood actors - and even formed an unlikely romantic pairing with Steve Coogan.

Now Courtney Love can add a supermodel to her list.

The 45-year-old singer has stunned the showbusiness world by claiming she had a lesbian fling with Kate Moss.

Best friends: Kate Moss and Courtney Love at the launch of a clothing collection by Stella McCartney in 2008 - the singer claims the pair enjoyed a sexual relationship in the Nineties

Miss Love, the widow of Kurt Cobain, the Nirvana lead singer, has hinted in the past that she had an affair with a leading model but has always refused to identify her.

But after being pressed during an interview with Irish magazine Hot Press, she claimed she had a brief fling with the 36-year-old supermodel in Milan in the Nineties.

'It was Kate Moss,' she said. 'She doesn't care. It's a great story for the grandchildren, so yeah.

'Kate wasn't doing a lot of drugs. It was just a thing that happened in Milan in the Nineties.

Claims: The controversial figure of Courtney Love walks down the street in New York earlier this week

'It happened and it was fun and whatever. And she talks about it and so I hope she doesn't get mad that I outed her about it. I feel like such a kiss-and-tell. Kate's great though, a good friend of mine. I almost bought her house in St John's Wood.'

Miss Love has angered several former lovers by giving a series of indiscreet interviews about her love life.

Last month, she claimed she had enjoyed an eight-month relationship with Gavin Rossdale, the husband of pop star Gwen Stefani, when he and Miss Stefani were dating.

Miss Love told a U.S. radio show the Bush frontman 'was lovely' during their alleged affair and that they 'had a really good time'.

Rossdale has not denied the allegation but issued a statement on his Facebook page, saying: 'All is well with the Rossdale family.'

Four years ago, she bizarrely claimed that she was expecting a child with Steve Coogan, best known for his comic alterego, the Norfolk radio show host Alan Partridge.

Coogan denied the allegations, saying they were 'just good friends'. Love, who lost custody of her 17-year-old daughter with Cobain to her late husband's mother last December, has also dated Hollywood actor Edward Norton.

She is believed to be in a relationship with comedian Noel Fielding, from the cult BBC series The Mighty Boosh.

Miss Moss has also had a turbulent love life. The model dated actor Johnny Depp for four years and was also engaged to drug-addict musician Pete Doherty.

But there have always been rumours that the list is not exclusively male.

Kate was of course a founding member of the notorious Primrose Hill set, alongside Sadie Frost, Jude Law, Davinia Taylor, Anna Friel and Pearl Lowe, to name just a few.

The tangled friendship group became notorious in the mid-1990s amid rumours of 'wife-swapping', uninhibited sexual antics and drug-taking.

She also shared a very close friendship with Jade Jagger, daughter of Rolling Stones frontman Mick. Laura Collins book, Kate Moss: The Complete Picture, detailed how the pair used enjoyed clubbing and skinny-dipping together in Ibiza.

Grand entrance: Kate Moss has a touch of the Amy Winehouses about her as she arrives with Sir Philip Green at Topshop's new flagship store in Knightsbridge

Jason Lake, Diana Dors' son had a fling with Kate on the party isle just after she split from Depp.

The pair were reknowned for going clubbing wearing next to nothing. Jason has said 'skin was definitely in.'

All three of them stayed at Jade's 'finca' [farmhouse] and the girls would often sunbathe nude and skinny dip together.

Anna Friel, famous for playing lesbian Beth Jordache in soap Brookside was also part of the notorious Primrose Hill scene.

Intense friendship: Jade and Kate at the Julien McDonald fashion in 2000

She and Kate holidayed together in Italy back in the 90s and in March 1999, Kate was in New York for Anna's opening night in Patrick Marber's play Closer.

The pair were so tactile that one photographer covering the party claimed his film was confiscated because the pictures were so 'hot'.

It was not the first time Kate's physically affectionate relationship with her female friends had been commented on.

She and Anna had already famously shared a playful kiss in The Groucho Club in London.

Bush singer Rossdale, 44, has links to the Primrose Hill set too - in 2004 a DNA test revealed that he was the father of Pearl Lowe's teen daughter Daisy.

He has not denied sleeping with Love while he was dating Stefani, with whom he has two children but issued a statement saying they were happily married.

Love claimed: 'Me and Gavin dated for eight months but never when they were engaged.

'He was playing the field. One night Gavin made roast chicken and it was really, really good. And then we snogged and he was really good at it.'

Love, who has lost custody of her 17-year-old daughter by Cobain, also had a high-profile fling with American History X actor Edward Norton.

Moss has a seven-year-old daughter, Lila Grace, with former boyfriend Jefferson Hack, a magazine executive.

The model, who launched her 12th fashion collection for Topshop on Tuesday, has been dating Jamie Hince from rock band The Kills since 2007.

Earlier this year, Italian Prince Emanuele Filiberto, a hedge fund manager, revealed he and Miss Moss had enjoyed a brief relationship after Marianne Faithfull brought them together in 2001 during a stay at his home on Corsica.

A spokesman for Miss Moss declined to comment.
