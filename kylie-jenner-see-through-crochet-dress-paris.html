Kylie Jenner Wears See-Through Crochet Dress in Paris

Kylie Jenner is currently in Paris doing fancy Paris things, including attending yesterday's Balmain show, where she showed up in the perfect sheer crochet dress.

MEGA//Getty Images

She paired the piece with see-through pointed-toe heels to match, and kept her beauty look rosy and fresh.

MEGA//Getty Images

Jenner's Paris outing comes after the second episode of Season 2 of The Kardashians aired on Hulu. During the episode, Jenner opened up about why she and Travis Scott changed their baby son's name from Wolf.

Speaking to mom Kris Jenner, the lip kit mogul said that she and Scott "think we know his official name," but don't want to reveal it just yet "because, God forbid, we change it again."

Jenner explained that she and Scott "thought [the name] was gonna just come to us when we saw him," but that didn't exactly happen. The problem? They had to submit a birth certificate ASAP, so they "just put Wolf Webster in that moment," a decision Jenner regrets. "Right after I signed the birth certificate, I was like, 'What did I just do?'" she told her mom, adding that her son's name "hasn't been changed legally yet," because "Travis actually still changes his name a few times."

"He'll come back and be like, 'I really like this name.' And then for the day, he'll call him that," Jenner explained. "And I'm like, 'We can't do this again.'"

Jenner and Scott's baby was born this February, so they've managed to keep his new name a secret for about seven months; however, fans have theories, including that his name is "Snowy," thanks to something Khloé Kardashian's daughter, True Thompson, once said.
