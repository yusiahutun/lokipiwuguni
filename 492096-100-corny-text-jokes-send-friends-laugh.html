100 corny text jokes to send to friends and make them laugh

Life is a journey that is never linear and should not be taken too seriously. There will be days when you feel like you can conquer the world, and others when waking up takes all your strength. On both days receiving corny text jokes from your friends would improve your day.

Whether you consider your friends serious people or not, there are some corny jokes that you would send to them and create a smile on their faces. Sometimes all it takes for a person's day to be made is one silly, corny joke from their friend.

Corny text jokes

Everyone has a unique and remarkable sense of humour, and you are fortunate if you share yours with your friends. So let them know you think of them by sharing these corny jokes that you may think are silly. Of course, it would tickle them if it's funny to you. After all, birds of a feather flock together.
