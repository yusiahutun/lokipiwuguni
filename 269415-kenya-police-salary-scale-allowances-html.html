Kenya Police salary in 2023: Salary scale and allowances

The Kenya Police salary and allowances depend on many factors, including educational level, years of experience, and rank. While part of the public believes the current wages match their heavy responsibilities, others think otherwise.

Since police officers are among the most respected professionals in the country, they have high status in every community. The salary of Kenya police was increased by 100% in 2015, and the government spread the increment in four stages until 2018, when pay cuts set in.

Kenya Police salary scale in 2023

There are 12 Kenya police job groups, F, G, H, I, J, K, L, M, N, P, R, and S. Police constables with only O-level certificates (or an equivalent KCSE mean grade) are in job group "F", while degree holders are in job group "J."

However, some cops holding high positions are not degree graduates because they joined the system years before the government enacted new policies.

Updates about the Kenya Police salary pay cuts

Graduate police/constables went to the streets in February 2022 to demonstrate against pay cuts made by the National Police Service Commission (NPSC).

The commission reduced the salaries, explaining that there was no rank as a graduate constable. As a result, two police officers sued the commission for reducing their pay, and about 1,000 also threatened to do the same after their November 2021 payslips indicated wage cuts.

The salary downgrade affects officers from all formations, including the Directorate of Criminal Investigations (DCI). Additionally, these deductions exceed the earnings of most officers and leave them with nil net pay.

Officers demanding salary increments in court lost the case after the High Court ruled that cops should not receive salary raises without the Salaries and Remuneration Commission's consent.

Although Section 83 of the National Police Service Act allows police officers to form associations that can table their reverences to the Inspector General and the NPSC, they cannot enjoy these fundamental rights and freedoms because their job is sensitive. Hence, they cannot join and participate in trade unions or go on strikes.

The High Court stopped NPSC from reducing the salaries of graduate inspectors in December 2020 until a petition filed by officers was over.

NPSC's proposed salary changes also included imposing taxes on disabled officers and graduate constables earning a basic salary equivalent to non-graduate inspectors with higher stipends because of their ranks.

In a recent memorandum delivered to the task force, the NPSC advocated for an upward review of police officer compensation, calling for a 79% rise in the base pay of a police constable to Ksh. 36,450 to make up for the extended period during which police officers had not received a pay increase.

In addition to the salary increase, the NPSC has recommended that the Inspector General of Police be rotated among three units of the Kenya Police Service (KPS), Administration Police (APS), and the Directorate of Criminal Investigations (DCI).

NPSC reviews the Kenya police salaries after every 2-4 years. As of 2023, the basic monthly salary of Kenyan CID officers ranges from Ksh. 32,880 (lowest) to Ksh. 854,241 (highest).

What is the AP salary in Kenya?

The basic AP and traffic police salary in Kenya range from Ksh 17,190 to Ksh 180,090. The Administrative Police collaborate with government agencies to enforce society security laws and promote development.

What is the police cadet salary in Kenya?

A police cadet salary earns a basic monthly salary of Ksh 38,900 without any allowances included.

How much do police recruits get paid?

The Kenya Police salary and allowances for constables/fresh graduates are way less than the wages of new graduates in other fields. They earn a basic monthly salary of Kshs 32,880. However, the amount might reduce to Ksh 18,760 if NPSC implements its proposed pay cuts.

What is the OCS salary in Kenya?

The salary of the OCS in Kenya varies depending on various factors such as years of experience and qualifications. However, according to the latest payscale data available, the average salary of an Officer Cadet in Kenya ranges from Ksh 70,000 to Ksh 120,000 per month.

What is the GSU salary in Kenya?

The GSu fall under group I, J, K and L. The basic salary they get ranges from Ksh 33,990 to Ksh 45,540.

The issue of Kenya Police salary and allowances has been a contentious one for many years. The low salaries and inadequate allowances have led to corruption and low morale among police officers, which ultimately affects the quality of service they provide to the public.
