Love at First Flight cast revealed

Eight singles are embarking on cross-country trips with total strangers in hopes of falling so head over heels in love that they say 'I do' at the end of their journeys.

The new Lifetime reality series Love at First Flight chronicles the adventures of four new couples who have been sent off on romantic — yet stressful — trips across North America.

After being paired together by professional matchmakers, the couples will meet for the first time on Tuesday night's premiere of the show. Although the participants come from all over the country, they have one thing in common: they are all unlucky in love.

The couples' compatibility will be tested with a series of travel-based challenges as they make their way from New York to Los Angeles. At the end of the trip, they must decide whether they want to get married or go their separate ways.

Alma Lopez and Michael Naccari

Alma, 31, from Chicago, Illinois, is social services case worker who loves fitness and going out dancing with friends. Although she is ready for marriage, she won't say 'I do' until she finds a man who is able to keep up with her in every way.

Family is important to the former beauty pageant contestant, who celebrates her Puerto Rican and Mexican heritage by visiting her relatives in Puerto Rico twice a year. 

Although she describes herself as 'strong-willed' and 'blunt,' she admits that she tends to fall for men who are either desperate and needy or emotionally stunted and unable to commit.

Out of the four serious relationships she has had in her lifetime, two ended up up in proposals. While one former boyfriend proposed to her after their relationship ended as an attempt to win her back, the other popped the question after just a few months of dating.  

Alma is looking for a man who as strong an independent as she is, and Michael may be her perfect match.

The 30-year-old accountant from Bellmore, New York, wants a woman who is comfortable in her own skin and willing to try to understand both his strengths and weaknesses.
