Zoe Colletti Biography, Age, Height, Boyfriend, Net Worth

Zoe Colletti, also known as Zoe Margeret Colletti is an American actress known for her roles in movies like “Annie”,” Wildlife” and “Scary stories to tell in the dark”. The actor was more recently seen in a pivotal role in season 6 of AMC’s show “Fear the walking dead”.

Early life

Zoe was born to her parents, Christopher Colletti and Cheryl Colletti on 27th November 2001. She was born in their hometown Burlington, New Jersey and belongs to a family of mixed ethnicities as her father, is of Italian descent and her mom belongs to Dutch-German genetics. Zoe is Christian and a citizen of the United States of America. Zoe also has an older brother named Ian Colletti. Her brother is a famous actor and singer known for his role in the movie Preacher. She developed an interest in acting through her brother and followed his footsteps into the film industry. Zoe is currently 19years old.

Zoe has not shared much about her education with the media or on her social media but it is believed that she has completed her school education from her home town Burlington in New Jersey where she spent most of her life. She also holds a degree as an undergraduate although her subjects of graduation or the name of university are unknown. As Zoe has managed to become such a talented and skilled actor at such a young age, it is believed that she took up several pieces of training and courses to help nurture her natural talent.

Zoe Colletti Career

Zoe wanted to pursue her career in the entertainment industry just like her brother and started off as a model. She later entered into acting when she secured her debut role in 2006 was cast in the Television comedy movie “American Men” by Rob Schiller in the role of Emma Wilson. Zoe later appeared in the brief role of Cydie in the 2010 movie “Mercy” and as Elena Moody in the series “Past life”. Meanwhile, over the years she performed in various TV shows as a dancer.

She got her first break though at the age of 13 when she was cast in the 2014 movie Annie as Tessie. Her performance was well appreciated and she became popular among the masses. She went on to secure another role in the TV movie “Nerd herd” in 2015 as Phoebe. In 2018 she bagged another successful role in the movie “Wildlife” as Ruth Anne. Her performance in the movie was highly praiseworthy. The same year she appeared in the main role of Lucy in the short film “Lucy in the sky”. The following year she appeared in the movie “skin” in the role of Desiree and in the series “city on a hill” as Kevin Bacon’s daughter.

The year 2019 was her most successful year as she appeared in the horror movie “scary stories to tell in the dark” She gained immense popularity after the movie. Her more recent appearance has been in “A boy called Christmas”, a 2020 film by Rob Schiller who has previously directed her debut film. She appeared in the role of The truth pixie in the movie. In 2021, she bagged the role of Dakota in season 6 of the television series “Fear the walking dead”. She has a pivotal role in moving the season forward.
