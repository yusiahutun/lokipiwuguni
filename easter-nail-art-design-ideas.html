32 Cute Easter Nail Art Design Ideas for 2024

1

Peeps-Inspired Easter Nail Idea

Bellacures

Swap regular French manicure tips for yellow ones for bright and happy springtime nails inspired by the popular Peeps marshmallow treats. See more designs at Bellacures.

2

Lovely Lilac Easter Nail Idea

Bellacures

The simple three-dot detail on solid nails works for any color combination any time of year, but we sure do love this pretty light pink and purple look for Easter. See more designs at Bellacures.

3

Peachy Keen Easter Nail Idea

Bellacures

With a light, peachy base polish and an energetic shot of orange alternating from the nail beds and tips, this simple design makes nails somehow appear longer. See more designs at Bellacures.

4

Baby Chick Easter Nail Idea

mtreasure//Getty Images

We absolutely adore this look for teens and tweens! This combo of pastel cosmic-inspired glitter nails with a hatching baby chick accent nail makes for a youthful Easter design for kids of all ages.

5

Perfect Stripes Easter Nail Idea

Oleksandr Smirnov//Getty Images

Pro tip: Use nail striping tape to achieve the clean lines of these multi-colored accent nails.

6

3D Cross Nail Charms Easter Nail Idea

6

3D Cross Nail Charms Easter Nail Idea

Apply bejeweled cross charms to an accent nail for a reminder of what Easter is all about.

7

Peeking Bunnies Easter Nail Idea

Christina Radcliffe//Getty Images

We are just so obsessed with this family of bunnies peeking in on each nail! Use nail polish pens instead of regular polish to draw on their heads, eyes, and ears.

8

Minimalist Easter Nail Idea

Dariia Chernenko//Getty Images

For this minimalist take on spring nails, start by painting on a nude or clear base. After picking one or two pastel polishes, paint half the nail (either the top or the bottom) or a clean stripe down the middle as shown. Let dry completely, then seal with a strong clear top coat.

9

Tiny Polka Dots Easter Nail Idea

Dariia Chernenko//Getty Images

The easiest way to achieve clean polka dots on nails is to use nail polish pens or a dotting tool. And remember to let the base coat dry completely first!

10

Flower Power Easter Nail Idea

sony_moon//Getty Images

Spring is in full bloom, so why not bring that same beauty to your manicure. Start with a clear base, as shown here, or a light pink would be pretty too. Layer on flower nail decals or paint on flowers and stems in whatever colors you desire. You can really get creative with this look!

11

Blue Bunny Easter Nail Idea

Denisfilm//Getty Images

Take a baby blue manicure to the next level by painting the ring fingers a slightly darker shade of blue, then embellishing with a white bunny. Don't forget the pink nose—that's what really makes it!

12

Peeps-Inspired Nail Decals Easter Nail Idea

12

Peeps-Inspired Nail Decals Easter Nail Idea

It doesn't get any easier than these super cute and colorful Peeps-like nail stickers. Talk about instant gratification!

13

Pink Polka Dots Easter Nail Idea

Black Lollipop//Getty Images

Simple pink polka dots on buttery nails is a classic Easter look, and one of the easiest to pull off too. Other color combinations we love: purple dots on baby pink nails; white dots on natural nails; pink dots on white nails.

14

French Manicure Easter Nail Idea

LemonSeed//Getty Images

A fresh, simple French manicure works for any occasion, including Easter. Whatever other social events come your way during the season—weddings, showers, graduation—you'll be all set!

15

Baby Chick with Stripes Easter Nail Idea

Christina Radcliffe//Getty Images

This baby chick design is easier than it looks. Start with a bright yellow oval, then draw in black eyes and wings, then finish with an orange beak and feet. Buy nail polish pens for clean edges.

16

Classic Easter Egg Designs Nail Art Idea

Christina Radcliffe//Getty Images

Take the classic lines and dots used to decorate Easter eggs to your nails for a super cute and classic design atop pink, green, yellow, and purple polish.

17

Speckled Eggs Easter Nail Idea

Christina Radcliffe//Getty Images

Borrow this design from those delicious speckled egg candies we all hope to find in our Easter baskets. Start with matte polish and dot on white and brown paint pens to mimic the look of the outer shell.

18

Spring Flowers and Egg Motif Easter Nail Idea

Christina Radcliffe//Getty Images

Whether you draw in your own zig-zags and flowers or buy flower nail decals online, borrow inspiration from this bright and happy design to re-create your own.

19

Perfectly Pastel Easter Nail Idea

Iryna Veklich//Getty Images

Take a rainbow approach to your next manicure. Featuring springy pastels, this happy look will take you well into April and May.

20

Bunny Tail Easter Nail Idea

Christina Radcliffe//Getty Images

We are squealing over that bunny tail! This adorable polka dot design, with a singular 3D dot mimicking a fluffy tail, is as good as it gets for Easter.
