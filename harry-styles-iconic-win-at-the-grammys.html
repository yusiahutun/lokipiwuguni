Harry Styles Iconic Win at the Grammys! Heres all You Need to Know About the Pop Icon

Heading over to a party at Harry’s House? Why not? Most of us are! Nothing now can remain As it Was, because the beloved pop star has bagged his trophy for Harry Styles album of the year at the Grammy Awards for his album “Harry’s House.”

“I’ve been so, so inspired by every artist in this category with me at a lot of different times in my life,” Harry Styles said as he accepted his trophy. “I think – like on nights like tonight – it’s obviously so important for us to remember that there is no such thing as best in music.”

Styles further added, “This doesn’t happen to people like me very often and this is so, so nice.”

While we are all gushing over Harry’s win last night, let us take a moment for the singers’ journey over the years.

Following the One Direction- Music!

This beloved Brit found his spotlight in 2010, after he tried out for The X Factor UK. Judge Simon Cowell later paired him with other contestants- Niall Horan, Liam Payne, Louis Tomlinson, and Zayn Malik, where they formed a boy band- One Direction.

The band’s career quickly took off and the five of them became household names in the UK, U.S and beyond! During their five-year band career, One Direction released five studio albums- Up All night, Take Me Home, Midnight Memories, Fours and Made in the A.M, after which they broke up in 2016 to pursue solo careers.

Harry’s Solo Career

In the June of 2016, Harry Styles and Columbia Records (the same label behind 1D) signed a solo contract. Following which Harry released his self-titled debut album about a year later which received critical acclaims. He then embarked on a world tour, also dropping Fine Line in December 2019, his sophomore effort.

The album featured singles- “Lights Up”, “Watermelon Sugar”, and “Adore You”. US Weekly had named it the No. 6 album of the year.

“Watermelon Sugar” made quite the groove, as this single won Harry his first ever Grammy in February 2021 for Best Pop Solo Performance, which he had performed at the top of the awards show.

Jumping straight to the Grammy’s 2022, where he won a trophy for ‘Album of the Year’ for hit record “Harry’s House.”

Harry’s House had debuted at No.1 on the Billboard Charts and the hit ‘As it Was’ charted at No.1 for as long as 15 consecutive weeks!

Now those numbers ought to bring home a Grammy!
