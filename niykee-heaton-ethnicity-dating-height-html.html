Niykee Heaton Plastic Surgery, Boyfriend, Net Worth

EntertainmentBy LRU Writer | On: July 02, 2019

Niykee Heaton made a name for herself in the entertainment industry with her music. She is a singer, songwriter, and model. The same year she got signed by a record label, her music topped the Billboard chart.

Today, know all about the singer's career, personal life, and more!

Niykee Heaton's Bio

Famously known as Niykee Heaton, her real name is Nicolet Aleta Heaton. She was born in Geneva, Illinois on the 4th of December 1994. 

Regarding her family, she is the middle child to her parents; mother, Veronica Heaton, and father, Joel R. Heaton.

For siblings, she had an older sister named Rachel Heaton. Unfortunately, she lost her life at the age of 21 due to liver cancer. Now, Niykee only has a brother named Cheyne Heaton.

As for her education, Niykee attended Geneva High School. Later, she landed a full scholarship at the University of Virginia. But, she dropped out of the institution to pursue her passion for music. 

Plastic Surgery Conterversaries 

Being involved in the entertainment industry, Niykee is aware that looks matters. Therefore, she opted to plastic surgery to change some aspects of her appearance.

Niykee Heaton: before (right) and after plastic surgery (Photo: plasticsurgerypeople.com)

Around 2016, the singer posted a photo with a plastic surgeon and, her fanbase went wild with speculation. There were talks of her having a nose job, lip enhancements, boob jobs, and a butt lift.

However, Niykee never confirmed anything on the matter.

It is only known that the model’s beauty comes from belonging to mixed ethnicity; she is of Welsh, Norwegian, English and German ancestry from her father’s side and South Africans for her mother’s.

Niykee stands with a height of 5 feet 8 inches (1.73 m) and a weight of 59 kg. Her body measurements scale in at 35-25-38 inches.

Is Niykee Dating? Boyfriend?

It may crush the hearts of millions, but the singer is not single. She is in a relationship with rapper, Jeff Logan. 

Jeff first made an appearance on the singer's social media, Instagram on 30th of December 2018. Since then, both of them regularly feature each other on their social media.

Niykee Heaton poses with boyfriend Jeff Logan on 14th of February 2019 (Photo: Niykee Heaton's Instagram)

Also, Niykee shared a photo of the couple on a beach on the 23rd of June 2019 showing how close they are with each other.

Career Details

Niykee is one of those people, who rose to fame with the internet platform, YouTube. Before stepping onto the mainstream entertainment scene, she used to make music video covers on YouTube as a high school student.

She rose to fame in December of 2012 when she covered Chief Keef’s hit songs Love Sosa. The video went viral. It gained more traction when WorldStarHipHop shared it on their website.

And, just like that, AllDef Digital signed the singer in 2014. The same year on the 24th of April, she topped Billboard’s Next Big Sound Chart. 

Out of her career as an entertainer, the singer has gained world-renowned fame. With her tours and her album sales, she must’ve also undoubtedly amassed an impressive net worth.
